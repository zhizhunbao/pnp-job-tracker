The government has [rules to protect foreign employees](https://yukon.ca/en/yukon-nominee-program-policy) in the Yukon Nominee Program.

Employers who don't follow these rules can be banned for up to 5 years. Bans put in place after September 1, 2015, are published on this page.

## Violation category definitions

- Type A. Minor violations like failing to sign the [Tri-partite Agreement](https://yukon.ca/en/yukon-nominee-program-sample-tri-partite-agreement) before the deadline.
- Type B. Moderate violations like not making all required deductions from a nominee’s pay.
- Type C. Serious violations like inadequate wages, fewer than minimum hours of work or receiving payment for accepting a nominee.

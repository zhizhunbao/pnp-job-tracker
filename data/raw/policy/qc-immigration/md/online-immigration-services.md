# Arrima - Online immigration and French learning services

Arrima is for:

- Individuals ;
- Employers ;
- [Representatives](https://www.quebec.ca/en/immigration/online-immigration-services/represent-person-arrima).

[Access to Arrima (in French only)](https://arrima.immigration-quebec.gouv.qc.ca/)

You can submit an application online for:

- The Programme de sélection des travailleurs qualifiés (Skilled Worker Selection Program);
- The Programme de l'expérience québécoise (Quebec Experience Program) –– starting at 8:30 a.m. Montreal time on July 2, 2026;
- The electronic submission of documents for students and foreign students;
- The collective sponsorship of a refugee abroad (suspended until December 31, 2029);
- The Programme des travailleurs étrangers temporaires (Temporary Foreign Worker Program) ;
- Accompagnement Québec;
- The Employer Portal;
- The outil comparatif québécois des diplômes étrangers (comparative tool for diplomas obtained abroad);
- French learning services for your workers during paid hours;
- A power of attorney.

If needed, consult the [tutorials](https://arrima.immigration-quebec.gouv.qc.ca/monespacepublic/aide/capsules) (in French) designed to facilitate your experience in Arrima.

You can also consult the platform’s [answers to technical questions](https://arrima.immigration-quebec.gouv.qc.ca/monespacepublic/aide/faq).

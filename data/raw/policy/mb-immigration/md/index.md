# Immigrate to Manitoba

Welcome to the official website of the Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program (MPNP), the Province of Manitoba’s economic immigration program for prospective Canadian permanent residents.

## Streams

### Skilled Worker Stream

If you have the skills and experience needed in Manitoba’s labour market, the **Skilled Worker Stream** may be for you.

### International Education Stream

If you are a recent Manitoba graduate, you may be eligible to apply through the **International Education Stream**.

### Business Investor Stream

If you are an investor or entrepreneur wanting to build a business or farm in Manitoba, the **Business Investor Stream** may provide you with a pathway to permanent residence.

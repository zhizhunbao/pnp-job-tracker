Before submitting personal information, verify that the website address matches one of the official websites listed above.

To help protect yourself online:

- Look for [https://](https://immigratemanitoba.com/2026/06/notice-to-mpnp-website-users) and the lock icon in your browser.
- Confirm the website address is correct and watch for misspellings or additional words in the domain name.
- Avoid clicking links received through unsolicited emails, text messages, or social media posts.

Please note that while the lock icon indicates your connection is encrypted, it does not by itself confirm that a website is legitimate.

If you encounter a website that appears to be impersonating the Manitoba Provincial Nominee Program, please report it using the webform at [https://immigratemanitoba.com/contact/](https://can01.safelinks.protection.outlook.com/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fimmigratemanitoba.com%2Fcontact%2F&data=05%7C02%7CDaniel.Stephens%40gov.mb.ca%7C0c11d560dfb6463c09b508decb292338%7Cabf64de92a5c4d77baa2a76265367d3a%7C0%7C0%7C639171574736048669%7CUnknown%7CTWFpbGZsb3d8eyJFbXB0eU1hcGkiOnRydWUsIlYiOiIwLjAuMDAwMCIsIlAiOiJXaW4zMiIsIkFOIjoiTWFpbCIsIldUIjoyfQ%3D%3D%7C0%7C%7C%7C&sdata=ad9DaR6IwbBT5MEUWASqbm3QF8E0AMjO6uKchCSZSt4%3D&reserved=0) and answer the following questions as indicated below:

- “What best describes your situation?”  Select “None of the above” from the pulldown menu
- “Inquiry type”  Select “I need to report suspected immigration fraud”

Please keep your message brief. Include the fraudulent website address and other key details.

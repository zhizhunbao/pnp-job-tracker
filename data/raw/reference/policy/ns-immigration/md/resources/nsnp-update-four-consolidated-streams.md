The Nova Scotia Nominee Program (NSNP) has modernized its immigration streams to create clearer pathways for newcomers and employers and to streamline processing.

## What has Changed

Ten NSNP immigration streams, each with different eligibility requirements based on occupation, work experience, education and language ability, have been consolidated into a new structure with four streams. As of February 18, 2026, the NSNP streams are:

- [Nova Scotia Graduate](https://liveinnovascotia.com/nova-scotia-graduate)
- [Skilled Worker](https://liveinnovascotia.com/skilled-worker)
- [Entrepreneur](https://liveinnovascotia.com/entrepreneur)
- [Nova Scotia: Express Entry](https://liveinnovascotia.com/express-entry)

## Eligibility Remains the Same

These streams include sub-criteria that mirror the eligibility requirements of the old streams.

### Skilled Worker:

- The Construction Worker sub-criteria are the same eligibility requirements as the former Critical Construction Worker Pilot.
- The Physician sub-criteria are the same eligibility requirements as the Physician Stream.

### Entrepreneur:

- The International Graduate sub-criteria are the same eligibility requirements as the International Graduate Entrepreneur Stream.

### Nova Scotia Graduate:

- The eligibility requirements for International Graduates in Demand are included in the new Nova Scotia Graduate Stream.

### Nova Scotia: Express Entry:

- The former Nova Scotia Experience: Express Entry, Labour Market Priorities and Labour Market Priorities for Physicians Streams have been consolidated into the new Nova Scotia: Express Entry Stream. This stream includes all the same eligibility requirements.

## New Submissions

- These changes apply only to new Expression of Interest (EOI) submissions submitted to the NSNP after February 18, 2026.
- The changes do not affect the Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP).

## Existing EOI Submissions

- EOI submissions already in the NSNP pool remain active and are not affected.
- The consolidation of NSNP streams does not affect your eligibility.
- No action is required from you.

We appreciate your patience as we continue to improve the efficiency and clarity of our immigration programs.

Our dedicated team can help answer your questions about living and working in Nova Scotia.

Community navigators are located across Nova Scotia and can help you determine the right community for you! We also have a team of employer engagement specialists to support companies and organizations looking to recruit foreign nationals and those living in other provinces in Canada. 

If you have specific questions about our programs, please reach out to our team.

## Immigration Fraud Reporting Tool

The Nova Scotia Department of Labour, Skills and Immigration (LSI) has made this reporting tool available for people to confidentially and anonymously report fraud or suspicious activity related to Nova Scotia immigration programs via Clearview Connects (Clearview).

- **Submit a report via the **[online portal](https://www.clearviewconnects.com/#/reporter/submit-report?org=PNSIPG&lang=en&vanity=true)**;**
- **Call using the toll-free number: 1-866-921-3145; or**
- **Mail a written report to:**

**ClearView Connects**
**PO Box 11017**
**Toronto, Ontario M1E 1N0**
[Submit a Fraud Report](https://www.clearviewconnects.com/#/reporter/submit-report?org=PNSIPG&lang=en&vanity=true)

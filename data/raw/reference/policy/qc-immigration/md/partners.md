# Integration support services offered by partner community organizations

About a hundred community organizations offer support to newcomers in their integration process in Quebec. This may include, for example, assistance in finding housing, support in the settlement process, information on obtaining essential documents or personalized, adapted and sustained support to develop independence.

You can benefit from these services as an immigrant, temporary foreign worker or holder of a residence permit for studies. Asylum seekers are only eligible for the housing search service and the part-time French course. (Please note that some elements in the dropdown menus are in French only.)

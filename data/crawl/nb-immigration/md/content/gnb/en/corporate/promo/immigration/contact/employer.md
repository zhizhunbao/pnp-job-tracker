**Employers who are currently utilizing provincial immigration programs or who currently have employees supported by work permits:**

Contact Immigration New Brunswick for specific enquiries about active immigration applications, about supporting your existing employees, or for general questions around immigration programs.

Write to [[email protected]](https://www2.gnb.ca/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#a2cbcfcfcbc5d0c3d6cbcdcce2c5ccc08cc1c3) and include the following:

- Be sure to write **Employer Enquiry** in the subject field of your email.
- Please specify your company name and **INB employer number**, if known, in your email.
- Please include your employee’s **INB candidate ID** if you are enquiring about an active immigration application.

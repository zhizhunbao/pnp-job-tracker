## Overview

The New Brunswick Express Entry stream is designed for highly skilled foreign nationals in the federal Express Entry pool. These individuals are looking to make New Brunswick their permanent home and contribute to the province’s growth. A nomination from ImmigrationNB will increase their chances of receiving an invitation to apply for permanent residency from Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).

## Targeted candidates

The New Brunswick Express Entry stream is intended for skilled foreign nationals who have the following connections to New Brunswick:

### Employment in New Brunswick

- You must be working full time in a non-seasonal position for a New Brunswick employer under TEER category 0, 1, 2, or 3.
- You must also have been working full time for the past 12 months under TEER category 0, 1, 2, or 3 for the same or a different New Brunswick employer while authorized to work in Canada.
- You have been living in New Brunswick for the past 12 months.

### New Brunswick Interests

- You have received a letter of interest in your federal Express Entry profile.

### Where to Study

New Brunswick’s universities are some of the oldest in Canada, and along with our colleges, have links to research opportunities as well as private industry. In addition, you’ll have the option to attend either a French-language or English-language institution.

### Working as an International Student

If you are a full-time post-secondary student enrolled at a Designated Learning Institution (DLI) you may be able to work while you are enrolled in school.

### Pathway to Permanent Residency

When you study here, you become part of our community. There may be opportunity for you to stay after you finish your studies and make New Brunswick your home.

### Study and Succeed in NB

Participate in a program that helps you integrate into the workforce.

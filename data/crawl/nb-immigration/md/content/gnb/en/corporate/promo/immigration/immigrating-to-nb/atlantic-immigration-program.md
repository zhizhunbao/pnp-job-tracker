## Overview

The Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP) is a federal-provincial initiative by the Government of Canada and the 4 Atlantic provinces, including New Brunswick. The program helps employers attract and retain skilled foreign workers and international graduates by supporting their applications for permanent residence with a valid job or job offer.

New Brunswick employers must be designated by Immigration New Brunswick (ImmigrationNB) to participate in the program. Once designated, employers can:

- hire qualified candidates for jobs they haven’t been able to fill locally with Canadian citizens or permanent residents
- apply to ImmigrationNB for an endorsement of each job offer and candidate

If the endorsement is approved, it can be used to support a permanent residence application.

Designated employers can also use the AIP to support foreign workers already employed in their organizations.

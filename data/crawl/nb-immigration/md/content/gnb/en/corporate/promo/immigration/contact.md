1. [Immigration](https://www2.gnb.ca/content/gnb/en/corporate/promo/immigration.html)

As New Brunswick’s official immigration team, we’re your experts for the clear, concise information you need to make the immigration process as easy as possible.

We’re part of the Government of New Brunswick’s [Department of Post-Secondary Education, Training & Labour](https://www2.gnb.ca/content/gnb/en/departments/post-secondary_education_training_and_labour.html). We work in partnership with employers, applicants, community groups, educational institutions, and many other stakeholders to create connections that enable immigration.

We know that immigrating to a new country isn’t easy and is a big step, but you are not alone. We’re here to make the process as easy as possible for you.

## Overview

This page provides information for foreign nationals who have received a nomination certificate under the New Brunswick Provincial Nominee Program (NBPNP).

It is also relevant to New Brunswick employers supporting a foreign national through any of the NBPNP streams.

Please navigate to the applicable section below and read the provided information carefully.

## Information for provincial nominees

> Important
> **If you have already received a nomination certificate from the Province of New Brunswick, withdrawing that certificate for the purpose of reapplying under another immigration program or stream administered by Immigration New Brunswick is not permitted.**

 A nominee under the New Brunswick Provincial Nominee Program (NBPNP) are expected to submit their application for permanent residence to Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) before their nomination certificate expires.

It is important to maintain the conditions of your nomination and notify ImmigrationNB of any changes in your employment situation.

## Information for employers

If you are an employer supporting a foreign national through any of New Brunswick’s economic immigration programs, you are required to inform ImmigrationNB of any changes concerning the employment of the said foreign national up until they receive permanent residence.

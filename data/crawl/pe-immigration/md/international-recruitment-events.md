# International Recruitment Events
 Published date: January 13, 2026  Share this page 

### Welcome to Prince Edward Island

Prince Edward Island (PEI) is situated on the east coast of Canada and is one of the four Atlantic Provinces. The PEI Office of Immigration is responsible for provincial immigration pathways to the province of PEI. Our mission is to support immigrants who choose to live and work in Prince Edward Island. We continue to focus on this by working with Island employers experiencing labour needs and skill gaps throughout the province while supporting the settlement and retention of newcomers to the Province.

PEI has much to offer with regards to lifestyle and work-life balance. We invite you to come study, work and explore our Island of endless beaches, wandering trails and unique experiences.

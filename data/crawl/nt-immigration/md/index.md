# Immigrate Here

> The Northwest Territories Nominee Program has closed its 2025 intake. The program has received enough applications to fill its nomination cap of 300 allocated by the Government of Canada.
> Applicants will be notified by email if their applications will be assessed or withdrawn.

**Ready to move to the NWT? We can’t wait to see you.**

If you’re looking for more information about what it’s like to live here, check out our [guide to life in the NWT](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/welcome-guide)[.](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/welcome-nwt)

To immigrate here through the Northwest Territories Nominee Program, you need to either:

- be ready to open, purchase or invest in a business in the NWT; or
- have a job offer from an employer in the NWT.

![Image of shoreline, Northwest Territories](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/sites/immigratenwt/files/website16.jpeg)

If you’re coming here to open, purchase or invest in a business in the NWT, you can apply for the Business Stream. If you have a job offer from an NWT employer, we call that the Employer-Driven Stream.

Select the option you would like to learn more about:

- [Business Stream](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/business-stream)
- [Employer-Driven Stream](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/employer-driven-stream)
- [Francophone Stream](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/francophone-stream)

Not sure what stream applies to you? [Contact us](https://www.immigratenwt.ca/contact-us) to speak to a real person in the NWT. We are here to help you

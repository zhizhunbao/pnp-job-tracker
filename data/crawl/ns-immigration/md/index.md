## Expression of Interest (EOI) Validity Period and Transition Measures
April 27, 2026 | Effective May 1, 2026, Nova Scotia will introduce a 12‑month validity period for Expressions of Interest (EOIs) as part of improved inventory management and program efficiency measures. To support a smooth transition, a phased approach will apply to EOIs currently in the system.[Program Updates](https://liveinnovascotia.com/taxonomy/term/3)

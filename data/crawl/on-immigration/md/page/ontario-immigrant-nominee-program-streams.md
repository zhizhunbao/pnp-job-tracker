# Ontario Immigrant Nominee Program Streams

Learn how to apply for a nomination for permanent residence in Ontario.

## On this page Skip this page navigation

---

## Regulatory amendments coming into force May 30

Please note that amendments to the regulations under the Ontario Immigration Act take effect May 30. These updates prepare for the redesign of the Ontario Immigrant Nominee Program (OINP). Please stay tuned to the [program updates page](https://www.ontario.ca/page/2026-ontario-immigrant-nominee-program-updates) for any further announcements.

## Overview

The Ontario Immigrant Nominee program (OINP) uses two intake systems to manage applications. Before you can apply to the program, you must register in either the Expression of Interest system or the Express Entry system to let the OINP know you wish to be invited to apply. The OINP has 9 streams you may qualify for.

## Stream Selector Tool

Use the OINP’s [Stream Selector Tool](https://www.ontarioimmigration.gov.on.ca/oinp_index/resources/app/home/index.html#!/selector) to identify which stream you may be eligible for based on your credentials. This tool is for information purposes only and it is your responsibility to ensure that you meet all stream criteria before applying by reviewing the relevant stream page.

## Ontario’s Expression of Interest Streams

The following streams operate through the [OINP’s Expression of Interest System](https://www.ontario.ca/page/register-expression-interest-ontario-immigrant-nominee-program). To qualify, you must register an expression of interest and receive an invitation to apply.

### If you have a job offer in Ontario

If you have a job offer in Ontario, you can apply to the following streams.

![A person shaking hands with another person at work](https://www.ontario.ca/files/2023-10/hand_shake.jpg)

#### Employer Job Offer: Foreign Worker stream

Apply for the [Employer Job Offer: Foreign Worker stream](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-employer-job-offer-foreign-worker-stream) if you are a skilled foreign worker with a job offer in Ontario or a physician.

#### Employer Job Offer: International Student stream

Apply for the [Employer Job Offer: International Student stream](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-employer-job-offer-international-student-stream) if you are a recent graduate with a job offer in Ontario.

#### Employer Job Offer: In-Demand Skills stream

Apply for the [Employer Job Offer: In Demand Skills stream](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-employer-job-offer-demand-skills-stream) if you are a foreign worker in an in-demand occupation with a job offer in Ontario.

### If you have a Masters or PhD degree from an Ontario university

If you have a Masters or PhD degree from an Ontario university, you can apply to the following streams.

![People standing in a row during a graduation](https://www.ontario.ca/files/2023-10/graduation.jpg)

#### Masters Graduate stream

Apply for the [Masters Graduate](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-masters-graduate-stream) stream if you have a masters degree from an Ontario university.

#### PhD Graduate stream

Apply for the [PhD Graduate stream](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-phd-graduate-stream) if you have a PhD degree from an Ontario university.

## Ontario’s Express Entry Streams

The following streams operate through the federal government’s Express Entry System. To qualify, you must have an [Express Entry profile and receive a notification of interest](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontarios-express-entry-system-streams) from Ontario.

### If you have the skills and experience that Ontario employers need

If you have the skills and experience that Ontario employers need you can apply to the following streams.

![A mechanic working on a car](https://www.ontario.ca/files/2023-10/working_on_car.jpg)

#### Human Capital Priorities stream

Apply for the [Human Capital Priorities stream](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontarios-express-entry-human-capital-priorities-stream) if you have the required skilled work experience, education and language abilities.

#### Skilled Trades stream

Apply for the [Skilled Trades stream](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontarios-express-entry-skilled-trades-stream) if you are a foreign worker with Ontario work experience in an eligible skilled trade.

#### French-Speaking Skilled Worker stream

Apply for the [French-Speaking Skilled Worker](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontarios-express-entry-french-speaking-skilled-worker-stream) stream if you are a French-speaking foreign worker with strong English language abilities.

## How to receive an invitation to apply or notification of interest

#### Under the OINP ’s Expression of Interest streams

- You must first register in the [Expression of Interest system](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-immigrant-nominee-program-expression-interest-system-streams) and receive an invitation to apply.
- Browse past rounds of [invitations to apply](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontario-immigrant-nominee-program-oinp-invitations-apply)

#### Under the federal Express Entry streams

- You must first register in the [Express Entry system](https://www.ontario.ca/page/ontarios-express-entry-system-streams) and receive a notification of interest before applying.
- Browse past [past notifications of interest rounds.](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-express-entry-notifications-interest)
- Learn about [Tech Draws](https://www.ontario.ca/page/oinp-tech-draws) under the Express Entry Human Capital Priorities stream

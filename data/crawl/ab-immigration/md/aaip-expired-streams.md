## Overview

The Alberta Advantage Immigration Program (AAIP) is subject to change over time. The following documents include information on AAIP streams, categories and initiatives that are now closed.

This information is intended to be a reference only for individuals who applied to the AAIP under these AAIP streams, categories or initiatives.

No further applications will be accepted for closed AAIP streams, categories and initiatives.

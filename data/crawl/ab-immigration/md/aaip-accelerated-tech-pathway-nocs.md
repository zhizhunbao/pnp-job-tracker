# Alberta Advantage Immigration Program — Accelerated Tech Pathway

Eligible National Occupational Classification (NOC 2021) codes (effective Nov 16, 2022).
Candidate's primary NOC in their Express Entry profile must match their current Alberta
employment or Alberta job offer; the employer's primary business must be in Alberta's tech
industry (per the AAIP tech NAICS list). Full-time employment / bona fide job offer required.

# Immigration

Services, programs and grants available to help you immigrate and settle in Alberta.

## A-Z
[Alberta Advantage Immigration Program](https://www.alberta.ca/alberta-advantage-immigration-program)

Eligible individuals can apply to be nominated to work and live in Alberta permanently.
[Alberta Immigrant Impact Awards](https://www.alberta.ca/alberta-immigrant-impact-awards)

Recognizing immigrants in Alberta who have made a difference.
[Alberta Immigrant Mentorship Innovation Grant](https://www.alberta.ca/alberta-immigrant-mentorship-innovation-grant)

Apply for funding for projects that support the development of career mentorship opportunities for immigrants.
[Alberta Settlement and Integration Program](https://www.alberta.ca/alberta-settlement-and-integration-program)

Eligible organizations can apply for funding to create services and initiatives that help newcomers settle and integrate in Alberta.
[Foreign Qualification Recognition](https://www.alberta.ca/foreign-qualification-recognition)

Newcomers to Alberta can learn how to get licensed to work in a regulated occupation.
[International Qualifications Assessment Service (IQAS)](https://www.alberta.ca/international-qualifications-assessment)

Get your foreign-earned credentials assessed for immigration or work and study.
[Refugee supports](https://www.alberta.ca/support-programs-refugees)

Resettlement, funding and other supports to help refugees settle in Alberta.
[Resources for immigrant workers](https://www.alberta.ca/resources-immigrant-workers)

Online tools, videos, statistics and other resources for workers moving to Alberta.

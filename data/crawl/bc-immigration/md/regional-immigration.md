# Regional Immigration Collaboration

Last updated: **May 8, 2026**
 
 The BC PNP's Regional Immigration Collaboration (RIC) is an initiative that supports the economic development strategies of communites outside the Metro Vancouver Regional District through immigration. By participating in the RIC, communities can play an active role in attracting and retaining workers and entrepreneurs wishing to make a smaller B.C. community their home.
 
 Communities interested in learning more about the RIC can contact the BC PNP by email at [PNPConcierge@gov.bc.ca](mailto:PNPConcierge@gov.bc.ca).

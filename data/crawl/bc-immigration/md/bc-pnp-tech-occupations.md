# BC PNP priority tech occupations (NOC 2021)

As of 2026 the dedicated **Tech draws closed (Dec 2024)**, but every occupation on the
former technology list remains eligible. Tech workers now apply through **BC Skills
Immigration** with a qualifying B.C. job offer (≥1 year, full-time) and a competitive
SIRS score — there is no separate tech pathway or tech-specific cut-off to target.

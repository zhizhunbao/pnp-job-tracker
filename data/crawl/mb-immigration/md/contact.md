**If you have applied or been nominated through the Skilled Worker Stream or International Education Stream, please submit your inquiry using the [Skilled Worker Webform](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/skilled-worker/webform/).

For all other inquiries, please use the form below.

If you have questions about your application for a visa or temporary permit or your application for permanent residence, please contact [Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC)](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/corporate/contact-ircc.html).**

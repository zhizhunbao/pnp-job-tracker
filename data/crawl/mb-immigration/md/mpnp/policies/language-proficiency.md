**Job-ready official language proficiency is required to apply to immigrate to Manitoba, Canada through the MPNP.**

To demonstrate your level of English proficiency, all applicants (except those currently working in Manitoba in TEER 0, 1, 2, or 3; previously NOC 0, A or B) must submit valid official results of an MPNP-approved language test taken no more than two years prior to the date you submit your MPNP Online application.

The MPNP-approved language proficiency tests are:

- [IELTS General Training](https://ielts.org/take-a-test/test-types) (English)
- [CELPIP – General](https://www.celpip.ca/take-celpip/overview/) (English)
- [PTE – Core](https://www.pearsonpte.com/pte-core/) (English)
- [TEF Canada](https://www.afmanitoba.ca/en/exams/tef/) (French)
- [TCF Canada](https://www.france-education-international.fr/test/tcf-canada) (French)

To measure your English or French levels, we use the:

- [Canadian Language Benchmarks](https://www.language.ca/resourcesexpertise/on-clb/) (CLB) for English
- [Niveaux de compétence linguistique canadiens](https://www.language.ca/ressourcesexpertise/niveaux-de-competence-linguistique-canadiens/) (NCLC) for French

**Note:** Applicants for semi-skilled and lower-skilled occupations (TEER 4 and 5; previously NOC C and D) must achieve a minimum of CLB 4 in English or NCLC 4 in French and submit their test results with their MPNP application. TEER 4 and 5 jobs include lower-skilled workers in the trades, primary and manufacturing industries, sales and services, and certain clerical and assistant categories.

## CLB/NCLC calculators

Use a CLB calculator to find out your CLB from your test scores. No calculator is currently available for PTE – Core or TCF Canada.

- [IELTS General Training](https://immigratemanitoba.com/calculators/ielts_calculator.php)
- [CELPIP – General](https://immigratemanitoba.com/calculators/celpip-g_calculator.php)
- [TEF Canada](https://immigratemanitoba.com/calculators/tef_calculator.php)

Calculators are provided for ease of use, employing the same test scores and CLB or NCLC figures as this page, which is the official program document.

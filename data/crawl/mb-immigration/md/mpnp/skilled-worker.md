The **Skilled Worker Stream** is locally driven and based on the needs of Manitoba employers. We select internationally trained and experienced workers who have the skills needed in the local labour market, and nominate them to receive Canadian permanent residence to settle and work in Manitoba.

There are two pathways: **Skilled Worker in Manitoba (SWM)** and **Skilled Worker Overseas (SWO)**.

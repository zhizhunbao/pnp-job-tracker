This process applies to the pathways under the **[Skilled Worker Stream](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/skilled-worker/)** and the **[International Education Stream](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/ies/)**, except for the International Student Entrepreneur Pilot. The pathways under the **[Business Investor Stream](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/bis/)** have a different, separate process that do not use the MPNP Online portal.

1. Create an [Expression of Interest (EOI)](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/apply/eoi/) profile.
2. If you receive a Letter of Advice to Apply (LAA), [submit a full application](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/apply/laa/) to the MPNP.
3. Applications are thoroughly [assessed](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/apply/assessment/) by an MPNP officer.
4. Successful applicants are [nominated by the MPNP](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/apply/nomination/).
5. Within 180 days from the date of nomination, make a separate application to the Government of Canada for [permanent residence](https://immigratemanitoba.com/mpnp/apply/pr/) for you and your family.

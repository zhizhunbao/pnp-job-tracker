## Overview

### 1. Nominations by Stream

- **6,367** total nominations across all program streams  1.5% increase from 2021
- **6,357** Skilled Worker nominations across all program streams  82% of Skilled Worker nominees had a job or job offer at the time of nomination 38% of Skilled Worker nominees completed post-secondary education in Manitoba
- **10** Business nominations67% decrease from 2021  $2.90 million invested in Manitoba by Business Investor nominees 20 jobs created/maintained in Manitoba

![](https://immigratemanitoba.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/graph.png)

| Stream | Applications Received | Nominations | Refusals | Total Processed* | Approval Rate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skilled Worker in Manitoba | 3,970 | 4,552 | 28 | 4,598 | 99% |
| International Education | 684 | 405 | 0 | 405 | 100% |
| Skilled Worker Overseas | 1,020 | 1,400 | 298 | 1,687 | 82% |
| Business Investor | 23 | 10** | –* | 24 | 78% |
| Total | 5,697 | 6,367 | 326 | 6,714 | 95% |
*Total Processed includes applications that were withdrawn by the applicant.**Due to the temporary-to-permanent (TR) model of the Entrepreneur Pathway (EP), the number of nominations differ from the number of approvals. EP applicants first receive an Approval in Principle and are then provided a work permit support letter. After the EP applicant has arrived in Manitoba on a work permit, invested in their intended business, and fulfilled the conditions in their Business Performance Agreement, they will then be nominated. There were 21 applications Approved in Principle in 2022.With the Farm Investor Pathway (FIP), which is a direct nomination model with a Deposit Agreement, FIP applicants are approved and nominated, arrive in Manitoba as a permanent resident, then invest in their intended business.

## Additional Details

### 9. Processing Times

The MPNP’s commitment to assesses complete applications is within 6 months. Applications which are not complete or with Expressions of Interest discrepancies may exceed this processing standard.

#### Overall Average

| Stream | Approved Applications | Refused Applications | Overall Average |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skilled Worker in Manitoba | 129 days | 263 days | 129 days |
| International Education | 90 days | N/A | 90 days |
| Skilled Worker Overseas | 139 days | 290 days | 172 days |
| Business Investor | 197 days | 155 days | 192 days |
Average processing times are calculated based on date of submission to decision.

## Overview

### 1. Nominations by Stream

- **6,275** total nominations across all program streams  11% increase from 2020
- **4,470** Skilled Worker nominations across all program streams  82% of Skilled Worker nominees had a job or job offer at the time of nomination 38% of Skilled Worker nominees completed post-secondary education in Manitoba
- **6** Business nominations73% decrease from 2020  $6.77 million invested in Manitoba by Business Investor nominees 28 jobs created/maintained in Manitoba

![](https://immigratemanitoba.com/wp-content/uploads/2022/08/mpnp-annual-report-2021-chart-1.png)

| Stream | Applications Received | Nominations | Refusals | Total Processed* | Approval Rate |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skilled Worker in Manitoba | 5,336 | 4,470 | 23 | 4,515 | 99% |
| International Education | 505 | 310 | 4 | 314 | 99% |
| Skilled Worker Overseas | 2,459 | 1,489 | 24 | 1,530 | 97% |
| Business Investor | 33 | 6** | 9 | 46 | 78% |
| Total | 8,333 | 6,275 | 60 | 6,405 | 98% |
*Total Processed includes applications that were withdrawn by the applicant.**Due to the temporary-to-permanent (TR) model of the Entrepreneur Pathway (EP), the number of nominations differ from the number of approvals. EP applicants first receive an Approval in Principle and are then provided a work permit support letter. After the EP applicant has arrived in Manitoba on a work permit, invested in their intended business, and fulfilled the conditions in their Business Performance Agreement, they will then be nominated. There were 36 applications Approved in Principle in 2021.With the Farm Investor Pathway (FIP), which is a direct nomination model with a Deposit Agreement, FIP applicants are approved and nominated, arrive in Manitoba as a permanent resident, then invest in their intended business.

#### B. Business Nominees

In 2021, there were **6** Business Investor nominees from the Entrepreneur Pathway and Farm Investment Pathway. Those under the Entrepreneur Pathway were confirmed to have created a new business or acquired an existing business based on their Business Performance Agreement. Those under the Farm Pathway were directly nominated and have identified potential locations for their farming business. However, as no community received five or more BIS nominees and counts of fewer than five nominees are suppressed to protect nominee identity, Business Investor destinations are not listed here.

## Additional Details

### 9. Processing Times

The MPNP assesses complete applications within 6 months. Applications which are not complete or with Expressions of Interest discrepancies may exceed this processing standard.

#### Overall Average

| Stream | Approved Applications | Refused Applications | Overall Average |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Skilled Worker in Manitoba | 58 days | 189 days | 59 days |
| International Education | 49 days | 215 days | 52 days |
| Skilled Worker Overseas | 64 days | 168 days | 66 days |
| Business Investor | 301 days | 196 days | 282 days |
Average processing times are calculated based on date of submission to decision.

[Français](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/apercu-du-programme-des-candidats-de-la-province-de-terre-neuve-et-labrador-pcptnl/)

The Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program (NLPNP) helps:

- **Employers **with labour market challenges.
- **Skilled workers, international graduates, and entrepreneurs **wanting to settle in Newfoundland and Labrador.

The NLPNP aims to attract individuals who can boost the province’s economy and settle permanently. Successful applicants may become permanent residents of Canada.

For Employers:

- **Labour Market Development Officers **can help find workforce solutions and assist with Job Vacancy Assessment (JVA) applications.
- **Contact**: Call 1-800-563-6600 or email [EmploymentPrograms@gov.nl.ca](mailto:EmploymentPrograms@gov.nl.ca).
- **More info**: [Employment Services](https://www.gov.nl.ca/jig/empservices/foremployers/)

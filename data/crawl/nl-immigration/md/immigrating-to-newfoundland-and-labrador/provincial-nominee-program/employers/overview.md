## Want to Hire International Talent in Newfoundland and Labrador?

If you’re an employer in Newfoundland and Labrador looking to fill full-time, long-term jobs, we can help you connect with skilled international workers and students.

The Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism offers free info sessions tailored to your needs. Whether you’re a business, community group, or stakeholder, we’ll walk you through:

- How to hire and keep international talent
- How to use Newfoundland and Labrador’s immigration programs
- Other ways to find the right people for your team

You can book a virtual or in-person session by emailing: [immigration@gov.nl.ca](mailto:immigration@gov.nl.ca)

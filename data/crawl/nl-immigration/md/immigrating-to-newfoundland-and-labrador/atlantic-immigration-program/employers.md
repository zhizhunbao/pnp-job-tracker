**Atlantic Immigration Program Forms and Application Guides**

Hiring international workers through the Atlantic Immigration Program is a three-step process. The Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism encourages interested employers to read the [Designation Guidelines](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/files/EN-Employer-Designation-Guidelines-fall-2023-review.pdf) and the [Endorsement Guidelines](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/files/EN-Endorsement-Application-Guidelines-fall-2023-review.pdf) prior to completing the designation application, to ensure the program fits employers’ needs.

**Use the Immigration Accelerator to:**

- Submit and manage AIP **Designation** applications
- Invite employees to submit an **Expression of Interest** for consideration as a potential Endorsee
- Submit and manage AIP **Endorsement** applications

Please note that the Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism moved to an online application system on May 17, 2023. All AIP applications must be submitted online through the Immigration Accelerator Portal.
[Immigration AcceleratorOnline Application](https://apps.gov.nl.ca/immigration-accelerator/home/)

**STEP 3: INTERNATIONAL WORKER IMMIGRATION APPLICATION**

**To apply for permanent residency foreign workers must:**

- Have a valid endorsement certificate issued by the province
- International workers who require a work permit must have a work permit referral letter issued by the Province

Employers supporting work permit applications must register in the [federal employer portal](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/corporate/partners-service-providers/employer-portal.html), submit the job offer and pay a compliance fee.

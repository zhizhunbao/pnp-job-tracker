[Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration)[Immigrating to Newfoundland and Labrador](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/immigrating-to-newfoundland-and-labrador/)[Atlantic Immigration Program](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/immigrating-to-newfoundland-and-labrador/atlantic-immigration-program/overview/)Designated Employers

To use the Atlantic Immigration Program (AIP), employers must be designated by the Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism (OIM). The requirements for designation can be found [here](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/files/EN-Employer-Designation-Guidelines-fall-2023-review.pdf). Once designated, employers can submit applications for their existing or prospective hires to OIM for endorsement. Once endorsed, the current or prospective hire can apply for permanent residence and, if necessary, a temporary work permit.

Key terms:

“**o/a**“: Operating as

**NAICS**: Business classification system by sector

**NOC**: Job classification by duties

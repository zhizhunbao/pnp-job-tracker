[Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration)2026 Commemorative Days for Newfoundland and Labrador Communities

The calendar was developed through input from various community groups and organizations in Newfoundland and Labrador, and in consultation with the Government of Newfoundland and Labrador IDEA Committee and the Department of Canadian Heritage.

The dates of celebration may vary based on local customs, according to moon sightings or solar calendar. If any dates noted here are incorrect, or if you would like to add new dates for the 2026 edition of the calendar, please e-mail us at [diversity@gov.nl.ca](mailto:diversity@gov.nl.ca).

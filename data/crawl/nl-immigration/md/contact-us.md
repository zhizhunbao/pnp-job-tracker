[Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration)Contact Us

For enquires regarding the Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program, please contact us via: [pnp@gov.nl.ca](mailto:pnp@gov.nl.ca)

For enquires regarding the Newfoundland and Labrador Atlantic Immigration Program, please contact us via: [aip@gov.nl.ca](mailto:aip@gov.nl.ca)

For enquiries regarding Settlement and Integration, Multiculturalism, or Diversity, please contact us via: [diversity@gov.nl.ca](mailto:diversity@gov.nl.ca)

For all other immigration related enquiries, please contact us via: [immigration@gov.nl.ca](mailto:immigration@gov.nl.ca)

Telephone: 1.709.729.6607; Facsimile: 1.709.729.7381

If you are an employer seeking assistance with a Job Vacancy Assessment, or if you would like to apply for Designation under the Atlantic Immigration Program, please contact 1-800-563-6600 or [EmploymentPrograms@gov.nl.ca](mailto:EmploymentPrograms@gov.nl.ca).

If you are experiencing technical difficulties with the Immigration Accelerator portal or any Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism (OIM) webpages (for example, login issues, error messages, or pages not loading), please contact [OIMDigitalSystems@gov.nl.ca](mailto:OIMDigitalSystems@gov.nl.ca)

**Please note that emails and phone calls will be answered during office hours: 8:30am-4:30pm, Monday through Friday.**

[Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration)Invitations to Apply (ITA) Updates

On February 19, 2025, the Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism (OIM) announced the launch of the [Expression of Interest Model](https://www.gov.nl.ca/releases/2025/ipgs/0219n02/)(EOI) for all economic immigration applications.

Frequently Asked Questions surrounding the EOI Model can be found [here](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/expression-of-interest-model-faqs/). OIM uses [EOI prioritization criteria](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/expression-of-interest-model-prioritization-criteria/) to guide its decisions.

OIM reviews EOIs and issues Invitations to Apply (ITA) in batches throughout the year. When ITAs are issued, the table below will be updated.

### Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program Independent Audit Report (2025)

Under the Canada-Newfoundland and Labrador Immigration Agreement, Newfoundland and Labrador is responsible for completing an audit of assessment and nomination activities in the Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program. The [compliance audit report](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/files/Audit-of-the-Newfoundland-and-Labrador-Provincial-Nominee-Program-Final-Report-Publication-Ready-January-26-2026.pdf), completed in 2025, represents an external, independent review of the Newfoundland and Labrador Provincial Nominee Program.

### Report on the Settlement Outcomes Survey (2020)

The Newfoundland and Labrador Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism, through the services of Goss Gilroy Inc., conducted an online Settlement Outcomes Survey during the months of February and March, 2020. The information gathered in this [Report on the Settlement Outcomes Survey](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/files/Goss-Gilroy-Settlement-Survey-Report.pdf) assists the Office of Immigration and Multiculturalism and partners in better understanding the settlement experiences and use of settlement programs by newcomers to Newfoundland and Labrador.

**Newfoundland and Labrador Multiculturalism Grant Program (NLMGP) **

The Newfoundland and Labrador Multiculturalism Grant Program (NLMGP) is a funding program that provides financial assistance to support eligible applicants as they develop, plan, and host initiatives that promote and celebrate cultural diversity in Newfoundland and Labrador.

The NLMGP provides funding to non-profit organizations and community groups, Indigenous governments and organizations, municipalities, and public education institutions to plan and host events celebrating cultural diversity; deepen intercultural and interfaith understanding; and promote public awareness of and participation in cultural events, celebrations, and holidays. Special consideration is given to initiatives focusing on equity, diversity, and inclusion.

**The objectives of the NLMGP are to:**

- Celebrate and contribute to cultural diversity in Newfoundland and Labrador;

- Promote awareness and participation in cultural events, celebrations, and holidays across the province;

- Increase awareness and understanding of cultural differences in Newfoundland and Labrador communities;

- Promote efforts to confront and address racism and discrimination through public education and awareness;

- Deepen intercultural and interfaith understanding within and between population groups;

- Create avenues for cultural groups and organizations to educate other residents about their specific cultures and to engage in culture-specific celebrations;

- Promote and encourage local understanding of newcomers’ positive contributions to the society, culture, and economy of Newfoundland and Labrador.

**Application deadline:**

- Applications for the 2025/26 NLMGP will be accepted beginning December 19, 2025 and will be accepted until January 30, 2026.

*Applications may be accepted on a rolling basis until funding is exhausted.

As the total funding allocation for this program is limited, applicants are encouraged to submit their applications as early as possible.

**How Do I Apply?**

All applicants are encouraged to thoroughly review the funding guidelines prior to submitting an application for funding. You can find these guidelines [here](https://www.gov.nl.ca/immigration/newfoundland-and-labrador-multiculturalism-grant-program-nlmgp-funding-guidelines-2025-26/).

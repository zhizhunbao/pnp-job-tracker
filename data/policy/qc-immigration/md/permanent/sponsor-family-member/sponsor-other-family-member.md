# Sponsoring another member of your family
 Procedures to follow if you want to help another member of your family immigrate to Québec. 

**Important notice**

The decision currently in effect regarding the management of applications under family reunification will end on June 25, 2026.

A new decision will be published in the Gazette officielle on June 23, 2026. The information contained on this page will be updated on that date.

For more information, consult [this page](https://www.quebec.ca/en/news/actualites/detail/new-reception-period-undertaking-applications-family-reunification-category-71270).

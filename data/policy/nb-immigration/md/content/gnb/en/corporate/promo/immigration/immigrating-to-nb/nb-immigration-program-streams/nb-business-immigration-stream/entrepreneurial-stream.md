The Entrepreneurial Stream has been replaced by the **New Brunswick Business Immigration Stream**. The forms below are for existing clients of the Entrepreneurial Stream only and are not valid for the current and active New Brunswick Business Immigration Stream.

If you require more information regarding the forms below or if you need to contact us for reasons such as withdrawal of an application to the Entrepreneur Stream or refusals of permanent residence applications, please email [[email protected]](https://www2.gnb.ca/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#6a0f19471c0f2a0d040844090b).

What was previously known as the Population Growth Division is now known as Immigration NB. If you are required to submit physical documents, please use the following address:

Department of Post-Secondary Education, Training and Labour

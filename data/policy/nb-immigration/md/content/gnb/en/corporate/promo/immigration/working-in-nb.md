### Finding a Job in New Brunswick

If you want to work in New Brunswick, you can even start your job search while still in your home country.

### Key Sectors and In-Demand Jobs

If you want to work in New Brunswick, you can even start your job search while still in your home country.

### Employee Rights and Obligations

When you work in Canada, you have rights. Even if you are a temporary foreign worker, you’re protected by law and have the same rights as other employees.

### Regulated Occupations and Licensing

New Brunswick has a variety of regulated occupations. If you plan to work in a regulated occupation, you’ll have to be certified or licensed by the regulatory authority for that occupation.

## Settlement services in rural Manitoba

Rural and small centres offer services for newcomers to help orient them to the community, the resources and services available, as well as providing specific supports for newcomers, such as language supports, meet and greet events, youth programming, employment supports and more. For more information, please visit the various settlement offices in Manitoba:

- [Northwest Regional Immigrant Services Inc.](https://nwris.ca/)
- [Westman Immigrant Services](https://westmanimmigrantservices.ca/)
- [Eastman Immigrant Services](https://eastmanimmigrantservices.com/)
- [Regional Connections Immigrant Services](https://regionalconnections.ca/)
- [Neepawa and Area Immigrant Settlement Services](https://neepawasettlement.ca/)
- [Portage Learning and Literacy Centre](https://pllc.ca/programs/newcomer-settlement-services/)

## IRCC directory of newcomer services

Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC) offers a searchable directory of IRCC-funded newcomer services. Open the [IRCC Newcomer Services](https://ircc.canada.ca/english/newcomers/services/index.asp) page to navigate to services relevant to you.

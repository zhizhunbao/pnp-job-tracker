## Assessing your eligibility

Make sure you and your business:

- meet the [Yukon Business Nominee Program (YBNP) eligibility requirements;](https://yukon.ca/en/immigration/yukon-business-nominee-program/find-eligibility-requirements-apply-run-your-business) and
- have a minimum score of 65 points on the [assessment point grid](https://yukon.ca/en/yukon-business-nominee-program-assessment-point-grid).

Applications that meet the eligibility requirements and have a minimum score of 65 points enter a pool of potential candidates for 6 months if your business concept is approved. Meeting the eligibility requirements and the minimum score, however, does not guarantee acceptance into the program.

For details, consult the [Yukon Business Nominee Program (YBNP) Policy.](https://yukon.ca/en/yukon-business-nominee-program-policy)

### Language requirements

You must have a minimum of:

- level 6 on the [International English Language Testing System (IELTS)](https://ielts.org/) English language test; or
- level 4 on the [Test d’Evaluation de Français pour le Canada (TEF Canada)](https://www.alliance-francaise.ca/en/exams/tests/tef-canada) test.

To determine language equivalencies from designated testing organizations, view Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada’s [language test equivalency charts](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/corporate/publications-manuals/operational-bulletins-manuals/standard-requirements/language-requirements/test-equivalency-charts.html).

## How to apply

1. Complete the [application](https://yukon.ca/en/yukon-business-nominee-program-application-form).
2. Complete the [Use of a Representative form](https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/application/application-forms-guides/use-representative.html) (IMM 5676) if you're representing a client.
3. Submit the complete and original signed application.

## Next steps

1. Applications that meet the program's requirements and have a minimum score of 65 points enter a pool of potential candidates for 6 months.
2. We'll invite only candidates selected from the pool to submit documents to verify the information contained in the original application and assess your business plan.
3. We'll invite you for an exploratory trip and an in-person interview in the Yukon if you meet the requirements.
4. If we approve your application after the interview, we'll support you in getting a 2-year work permit from the federal government. This will help you start your new business in the Yukon. During this time, we expect you to establish yourself, your family and your business in the Yukon.
5. We'll support your permanent residency application to the federal government at the end of the 2-year work permit period if you meet the requirements.

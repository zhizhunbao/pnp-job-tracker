About the job:        Establishing and enforcing local standards/regulations based to company regulations, distribute and collect assets, regularly count warehouses, clean up assets of resigned employees, and regularly clean up old/obsolete assets and idle/redundant assets to ensure asset security, consistency between accounts and goods, and efficient utilization. Based on the local business requirements, submit asset purchase requirements to the procurement department, accept and inbound assets/accessories upon arrival, and print, issue, and paste labels to ensure the continuity of asset services.      Perform other duties as assigned.

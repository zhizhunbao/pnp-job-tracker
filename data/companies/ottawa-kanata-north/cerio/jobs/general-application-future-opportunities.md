We are always on the look out for individuals who are curious and want to make an impact on an ever-changing industry. If that sounds like you, please take a moment to submit your resume and introduce yourself to us for future opportunities that may become available.   
   
  At Cerio we are united by a shared commitment to our core values. We believe that by upholding these values, we can achieve our vision of providing our customers with the best possible solutions. Built on the foundation of a supercomputing underlay fabric, the Cerio platform provides standards-based, low-code composability services for the cost-effective and efficient management of AI/ML infrastructure.  
   
   Cerio is headquartered in Ottawa, Canada, with offices and projects spanning international markets, and Centers of Excellence in Europe and North America. Cerio, creating new scale economics for the AI and Cloud era, delivers an open systems platform for a more sustainable data center. Learn more at   cerio.io   and follow us   on   X   and   LinkedIn  .   
     
   Our innovation has no boundaries; we have excelled at working remotely and supporting a hybrid model with both in-person and remote work.  While most positions have full flexibility in working remote, some positions may require regular in-office presence in our Ottawa office, located in Canada’s largest technology park.

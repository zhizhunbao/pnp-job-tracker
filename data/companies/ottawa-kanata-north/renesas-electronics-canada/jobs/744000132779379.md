Semiconductors are the digital oil of the modern economy—powering AI, automation, and the connected world. As a Sales Operations Analyst at Renesas, you’ll be part of this transformation. You’ll streamline sales processes, deliver actionable data insights, and drive operational efficiency. This role requires strong analytical skills, problem-solving ability, and a solid understanding of the semiconductor industry   Sales Strategy Support: Contribute to the execution of sales strategies by aligning data, tools, and reporting with business goals.  Data Management &amp; Integrity: Maintain and map sales data across systems, ensuring accuracy and consistency across market segments.  Forecasting &amp; Planning: Support customer forecasting and collaborate with business teams to meet financial targets.  Pipeline &amp; Funnel Oversight: Monitor funnel health, support opportunity tracking, and drive progress within the sales funnel.  Performance Metrics &amp; Reporting: Build and manage dashboards, scorecards, and ad-hoc reports to track KPIs and drive accountability.  Gap Analysis &amp; Opportunity Modeling: Use funnel analytics to identify risks and gaps, enabling proactive actions and growth strategies.  Target Setting &amp; Tracking: Assist in defining and monitoring performance targets for design-in and distributor initiatives.  Sales Enablement &amp; Business Tools: Train sales on reporting tools, dashboards, and process changes. Develop and manage tools that support planning, reviews, and operational efficiency across teams.     Bachelor’s degree in business, IT, Engineering, or a related field.  Proven experience in sales operations, analytics, or business support—ideally within the semiconductor or electronics industry.  Advanced proficiency in Microsoft Excel (including Power Query) and Power BI; experience with CRM systems (e.g., Salesforce) is a plus.  Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with high attention to detail.  Proactive, anticipative mindset with a strong sense of ownership and business awareness.  Excellent communication and collaboration skills across cross-functional teams  Ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver under tight deadlines in a fast-paced, results-driven environment.  Solid understanding of semiconductor industry dynamics and sales processes is a plus.

Reporting to the Director of Design Engineering you will have responsibility for the design, verification and validation of power management IC’s. Your responsibilities encompass the full design cycle which spans product definition through IC validation.  Responsibilities  • Perform transistor-level design of power management IC’s  • Define and quantify tradeoffs among IC architectures and perform feasibility experiments  • Participate in product definition with Marketing and Applications engineers  • Oversee the work of mask layout staff  • Write design specifications, IC verification and validation plans  • Participate in test specification development and Test Engineering support  • Execute lab validation and datasheet compliance testing  • Support Application Engineering and Marketing    Expertise in:   · BCD design: Low-voltage CMOS (1V - 5V), High-voltage DMOS (12V-100V)  · Analog building blocks: references, amps, oscillators, LDO’s  · Switch-mode power conversion topologies  • Familiarity with Cadence design tools • Excellent communication skills   Preferred Skills and Experience   • Integrated-FET switching regulators and PMICs  • Behavioral modeling of analog and mixed signal circuits  • Feedback control systems  • Precision current sensing techniques  • Regulated or unregulated charge pump circuits  • High precision, rail-to-rail op-amps  • Automated bench testing  • Use of tracking systems for development milestones, tasks and bug resolution  • Experience working with customers, marketing, applications, test and product engineers through the product life cycle  • BSEE with 5+ years, MSEE with 3+ years, or PhD   The expected annual pay range for this position is €39,000–€52,000.

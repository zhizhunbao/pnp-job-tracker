We are seeking a Principal Engineer – Physical Design to lead implementation and optimization of low-power, chiplet-based MCU designs on cost-optimized mature process nodes. This role focuses on delivering high-quality, affordable microcontrollers by leveraging proven technologies and efficient design methodologies.   Job Description    Own end-to-end physical design for chiplet-based MCU SoCs, including floorplanning, placement, power planning, Signal integrity, routing, timing closure and physical verification  Apply best practices for mature-node design to achieve cost efficiency without compromising performance  Collaborate with architecture and packaging teams to enable seamless chiplet connectivity and inter-die interfaces.  Ensure physical integrity and manufacturability for multi-die systems using established packaging technologies  Implement low-power techniques such as clock gating, power gating, and leakage reduction tailored for mature nodes  Balance power, timing, and area trade-offs to meet stringent cost and efficiency targets  Work closely with RTL, verification, and DFT teams to ensure design convergence  Partner with packaging and test engineering for robust product delivery  Define best practices for physical design flows optimized for mature nodes and chiplet architectures  Evaluate tools and flows that enhance productivity and cost-effectiveness     BTech/MTech in Electrical/Electronic Engineering, Computer Engineering or Computer Science with 10+&#xa0;years of experience in ASIC/SoC physical design with strong focus on low-power and cost-optimized designs  Proven experience in mature-node technologies (e.g., 12nm, 22nm) and chiplet-based architectures  Expertise in Cadence/Synopsys P&amp;R tools and sign-off flows (STA, IR-drop, EM analysis)  Extensive experience dealing with power intent (UPF/CPF) and multi-voltage design  Excellent leadership and communication skills  Experience with AI/ML based flow development and/or physical implementation is desired  Experience with packaging standards for chiplet integration is a plus      Renesas is an embedded semiconductor solution provider driven by its Purpose ‘ To Make Our Lives Easier .’ As the industry’s leading expert in embedded processing with unmatched quality and system-level know-how, we have evolved to provide scalable and comprehensive semiconductor solutions for automotive, industrial, infrastructure, and IoT industries based on the broadest product portfolio, including High Performance Computing, Embedded Processing, Analog &amp; Connectivity, and Power.

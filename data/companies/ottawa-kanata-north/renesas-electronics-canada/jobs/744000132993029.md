We are looking for a highly motivated Working Student or Intern to join our Impedance Sensing team at Renesas. In this role, you will contribute to the development of cutting-edge robotics prototypes, working at the intersection of hardware and software. You will gain hands-on experience with real-world robotics systems, helping to build innovative solutions using Renesas platforms.  &#xa0;  Responsibilities:   Design and develop rapid prototypes of hardware (including PCB design, schematic capture, and board bring-up) and software (firmware, data processing) for robotics applications.  Implement and test proof-of-concept solutions for tactile sensing, motion control, and human–machine interaction using Renesas platforms.  Collaborate with cross-functional engineering and application teams to translate requirements into functional prototypes.  Conduct system integration, debugging, and validation of HW/SW modules in real-world robotics use cases (e.g., robotic hands, grippers, mobility systems).  Document design concepts, PCB layouts, experimental results, and prototype performance, supporting internal demos and customer-facing presentations.

The OneSource &amp; SAP Tax IT Analyst is a hands-on role within Renesas’ Enterprise Applications organization, supporting OneSource and SAP S/4HANA implementation and post go-live support for global tax processes. The role focuses on the functional support of OneSource as the primary tax engine integrated with SAP S/4HANA. 
 
This position requires strong ownership, structured problem solving, and the ability to work independently after appropriate training and ramp-up. The candidate will collaborate with tax business users, FI SAP Analysts, project teams, and cross-functional IT stakeholders to deliver stable, efficient, and sustainable tax technology solutions. This role also provides exposure to global business processes and the opportunity to contribute to a large-scale SAP S/4HANA implementation project.   Role and Responsibilities:    Support SAP S/4HANA implementation and post go-live operations for global tax processes, with OneSource as the primary tax engine solution.  Configure, maintain, and support OneSource, including tax rules, tax determination, mapping, master data support, and tax calculation validation.  Analyze and resolve tax-related incidents, interface issues, and SAP S/4HANA–OneSource integration issues.  Translate tax and finance business requirements into practical SAP S/4HANA and OneSource solutions, including change requests and enhancements.  Support testing, documentation, deployment readiness, user training, and post-deployment stabilization.  Coordinate with internal teams and external support partners to ensure tax-related deliverables are completed with quality and consistency.     Undergraduate degree in Finance, Accounting, Computer Science, Technology, or related field.  Minimum of 3 years of hands-on experience in SAP Finance, SAP S/4HANA, or tax solution implementation and support.  Experience supporting tax processes through OneSource or similar SAP-integrated tax engine solutions. Candidates lacking OneSource experience are expected to ramp up quickly and achieve independence within a short period.  Strong analytical, problem-solving, verbal, and written communication skills in English, with the ability to facilitate sessions with business and technical stakeholders.  Comfortable working in a hands-on role, including independent analysis, configuration support, incident resolution, testing, documentation, and stakeholder follow-up after ramp-up.  Required to obtain the OneSource Professional Certification during the probation period.    Good-to-Have Requirements:    Understanding SAP SD/MM sales and purchase processes and their integration with tax determination or external tax engine solutions.  Experience with SAP S/4HANA implementation, rollout, post go-live support, global tax determination, or tax compliance tools.  Knowledge of SAP FI integration, VAT/GST/sales tax processes, indirect tax determination, or global tax compliance requirements.    Interview Expectations:    The interview will be a two-way conversation where we explore your experience, interests, and how your skills align with the role and with Renesas as a company.  You will have the opportunity to discuss your background and experience related to SAP FI tax processes, OneSource, or similar tax engine solutions if applicable.  We will also explore how you approach analysis, problem solving, and complex system or process-based scenarios.  Strong knowledge and hands-on experience in OneSource will be considered as a significant advantage and an opportunity to stand out.    Work Location:  Hybrid arrangement in Penang, currently requiring one onsite day per week, with the possibility of increasing to up to three onsite days per week based on business needs. For other regions, the role is expected to be mainly remote, subject to business requirements.

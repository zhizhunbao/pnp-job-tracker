The Tactical Marketing Specialist is responsible for  day‑to‑day execution and support  of pricing, quotation, sampling, and system setup activities for the fastest growing division in Renesas, supporting AI customers. 
This role focuses on  operational accuracy, timely support, and cross‑functional coordination , working closely with Sales, Product Marketing, and Operations to ensure smooth commercial execution while following established policies and approval frameworks. Assist in the maintenance of Quarterly Price Books and Master Price Program (MPP), focused on data accuracy and integrity  Work with marketing to setup and maintain pricing curves  Provide analysis, feedback and guidance to product line marketing for customer contract price reviews  Handle pricing‑related queries and coordinate approvals as required    Samples &amp; Evaluation Support    Support management of product samples  Maintain sample orders, including handling holds, priorities, approvals, and customer queries.  Support enforcement of maximum sample quantities for auto‑approval.    Operational &amp; Cross‑Functional Support    Act as a support point for  Sales and internal teams  on commercial execution queries.  Coordinate with global stakeholders across regions to ensure timely execution and information flow.

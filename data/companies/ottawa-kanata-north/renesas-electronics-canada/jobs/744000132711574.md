As a System Engineer, you will define and validate system-level solutions for server power products in the growing AI server market. In this hardware system engineering role, you will interface with customers to define power products, IC requirements, and system specifications, generate validation plans, and develop reference designs to ensure robust integration of 48V IBC in server, telecom, and industrial systems. You will drive cross-functional teams to deliver differentiated, highly integrated power solutions.   Responsibilities:    Conduct market analysis and define power products and specifications for the 48V IBC market  Collaborate with customers, sales, product marketing, applications, design, and validation teams to define architecture and specifications  Define IC requirements (e.g., controller, driver, power stage) aligned with system needs  Design and develop proof‑of‑concepts, validation platforms, and reference designs  Investigate and validate advanced 48V DC/DC and integrated solutions  Develop simulation models, calculators, and datasheets for product release  Perform system-level analysis (power integrity, thermal performance, fault response)  Create application notes and reference designs for real-world use cases     BS or above in Electrical/Electronic Engineering with 5+ years’ experience  Strong expertise in power electronics (DC-DC converters)  Good understanding of power topologies, control loop theory, and stability analysis  Experience in IC definition and system integration  Hands-on experience with PCB schematic/layout and CAD tools  Familiarity with simulation tools (PSpice, SIMPLIS, etc.)  Strong skills in lab debugging and validation  Experience developing technical collateral (datasheets, user guides, training materials)  Self-driven, results-oriented, with strong cross-functional leadership   &#xa0;     Renesas is an embedded semiconductor solution provider driven by its Purpose ‘ To Make Our Lives Easier .’ As the industry’s leading expert in embedded processing with unmatched quality and system-level know-how, we have evolved to provide scalable and comprehensive semiconductor solutions for automotive, industrial, infrastructure, and IoT industries based on the broadest product portfolio, including High Performance Computing, Embedded Processing, Analog &amp; Connectivity, and Power.

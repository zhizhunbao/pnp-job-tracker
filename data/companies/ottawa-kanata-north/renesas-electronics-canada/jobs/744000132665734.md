The FPGA product line represents a critical growth initiative in the low-density FPGA market—a segment with significant untapped potential. We are seeking a&#xa0; Staff Product Marketing Engineer&#xa0; to lead strategic marketing efforts, drive roadmap alignment, and ensure successful execution of product launches. Key Responsibilities     Strategic Leadership:   Define and execute product marketing strategies for ForgeFPGA to capture market share in the low-density FPGA segment.  Align product roadmap with market trends, customer needs, and business objectives.     Go-to-Market Execution:   Develop and implement comprehensive launch plans for new products and features.  Collaborate with cross-functional teams (engineering, sales, operations) to ensure seamless execution.     Market Analysis &amp; Positioning:   Conduct competitive analysis and identify differentiation opportunities.  Craft compelling messaging and positioning that resonates with target customers.     Sales Enablement:   Create sales tools, training materials, and collateral to support global sales teams.  Drive adoption of marketing strategies across regional teams.     Performance &amp; Growth:   Monitor KPIs and market feedback to optimize strategies and accelerate revenue growth.  Identify new opportunities for expansion within the low-density FPGA market.        Education: &#xa0;Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, or related field; MBA preferred.   Experience:   8+ years in product marketing or related roles within the semiconductor or FPGA industry.  Proven track record of leading strategic marketing initiatives and successful product launches.     Skills:   Strong understanding of FPGA technology and market dynamics.  Exceptional communication, leadership, and cross-functional collaboration skills.  Ability to translate technical capabilities into customer value propositions.

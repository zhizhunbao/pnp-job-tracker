  Overview  
 This position is for an Assistant Controller. Reporting to the Director of Finance, you will be part of a team of dynamic professionals, working on a large variety of tasks. We are a high-growth company that encourages creative thinking. If you are looking to continue your career in the technology industry, are full of energy, like working in a fluid and fast-paced environment and have excellent attention to detail, then we should speak. 
   
  In This Role, You Will  
 
 Assist with the preparation of IFRS financial statements; 
 Assist with technical accounting analyses, procedures, and policies; 
 Ensure all accounts reconcile monthly; 
 Implement and maintain required internal controls; 
 Participate in external audits 
 Support and assist other Finance team members; 
 Ensure payroll is processed in a timely manner, including regulatory reporting and documentation. 
 Ensure that all statutory requirements are met including withholding payments, income tax, goods and services tax. 
 Special projects as assigned 
 
   
  What You’ll Bring to the Role  
 
 CPA designation or close to completion is a must; 
 Expert-level Microsoft Excel experience is a must; 
 Strong problem-solving ability is a must; 
 IFRS experience is preferred; 
 Experience using AI-enabled productivity and analytical tools (e.g., Claude) to enhance financial analysis, reporting, forecasting, process automation, and decision-making is a plus; 
 Previous supervisory experience is a plus; 
 Knowledge of Great Plains is a plus; 
 Knowledge of systems implementation is a plus; 
 Previous exposure to multi-currency transactions; 
 High attention to accuracy and detail in working processes and reports; 
 Able to multitask, work under pressure and meet tight deadlines; 
 History of thriving in a rapidly changing environment; 
 Excellent written and oral communication skills; 
 You must be able to work onsite two days per week, Tuesday &amp; Wednesday

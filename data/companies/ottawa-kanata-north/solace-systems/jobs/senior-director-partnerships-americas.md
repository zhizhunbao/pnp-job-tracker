  About the Role  
  Solace is seeking a dynamic Senior Director of Partnerships to lead and expand our partner ecosystem across the Americas. This executive role is pivotal in driving partner-sourced revenue, scaling joint go-to-market initiatives, and strengthening Solace’s position as the partner of choice for enterprise-grade event-driven solutions.  
   
  What You’ll Do    
 
 
 
    Partner Recruitment &amp; Ecosystem Growth:      Define and execute a North America partner strategy aligned with global   objectives  .   Identify  , recruit, and onboard high-impact systems integrators (GSIs and regional SIs) and solution providers whose practices align with Solace's real-time data and agentic AI platform.      
 
 
    Business Development:      Enable partners to build profitable Solace practices around real-time data, event-driven integration, and agentic AI. Drive joint selling, co-marketing, and partner-sourced pipeline growth across North America.      
 
 
    Operational Excellence:      Implement scalable partner programs including enablement, certification, incentives, and MDF. Establish KPIs and reporting frameworks to measure impact.      
 
 
    Cross-Functional Collaboration:      Align with Sales, Product, Marketing, and Customer Success to ensure seamless partner engagement and execution.      
 
 
    Leadership &amp; Enablement:      Lead regional partner managers and foster a culture of collaboration and accountability. Represent Solace at industry events and executive forums.      
 
 
 
   
  What You’ll Bring to the Role  
 
  5-10+ years in partnerships, business development, or channel leadership within enterprise tech.  
  Proven success in building and scaling partner programs across diverse geographies. Strong executive presence and ability to influence cross-functional stakeholders.  
  Experience with partner-led GTM models and driving measurable revenue outcomes.  
  Excellent communication, strategic thinking, and leadership skills.  
  Ability to work without sponsorship required.

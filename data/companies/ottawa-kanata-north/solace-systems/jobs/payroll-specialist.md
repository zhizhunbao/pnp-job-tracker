  About the Role  
 This position is for a Payroll Specialist. Reporting to the Director of Finance, you will be part of a team of dynamic professionals, working on a large variety of tasks. We are a high-growth company that encourages creative thinking. If you are looking to continue your career in the technology industry, are full of energy, like working in a fluid and fast-paced environment and have excellent attention to detail, then we should speak. 
   
  What You’ll Do    
 
 Manage end-to-end payroll processing, including calculation of wages, deductions, tax withholdings, and issuance of payments to employees and government agencies. 
 Ensure all payroll withholdings/remittances are accurately processed and filed in a timely manner. 
 Process Canadian and international payrolls while working with local payroll providers in foreign jurisdictions to ensure compliance with local government regulations and company policies. 
 Act as a trusted advisor, providing payroll insights across the organization, and leading ad-hoc cross-functional payroll projects. 
 
 
 Process, record, and reconcile payroll transactions in the accounting system, including WSIB, EHT, and other payroll-related accruals. 
 Maintain accurate financial records, including journal entries, general ledger entries, and account reconciliations. 
 Lead any payroll audits and assist with financial audits as required. 
 Assist with period-end closing procedures, including month-end payroll accruals and performing variance analysis of month-to-month changes. 
 
   
  Required Skills  
 
 3+ years’ of work experience in a payroll and accounting role. 
 Strong accounting knowledge and proven ability to record payroll transactions. 
 Advanced Microsoft Excel experience (pivot tables, vlookups, hlookups, and advanced functions). 
 High attention to accuracy and detail in working processes and reports. 
 Ability to multitask, work under pressure and meet tight deadlines. 
 Excellent written and oral communication skills. 
 
   
  Desired Skills and Certifications  
 
 Payroll related certification (CPM, PCP) is an asset. 
 Experience with International payroll highly desired. 
 Educational background in accounting is an asset. 
 Experience using Great Plains or similar accounting system.

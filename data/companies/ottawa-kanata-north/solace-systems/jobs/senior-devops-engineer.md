  About the Role  The  Solace Platform Engineering team  is looking for a  Senior DevOps Engineer  to help design, build, and evolve the CI/CD pipelines, developer tooling, and development environments that power Solace Cloud and its supporting microservices.   
 In this role, you will operate at the intersection of platform engineering, DevOps, and developer experience, building scalable, secure, and reliable systems that enable product teams to move faster with confidence. You’ll work deeply with AWS, Kubernetes, Infrastructure as Code, and CI/CD systems, partnering closely with software engineers, SREs, and security teams to define best practices and improve building and delivering software at Solace. 
   What You’ll Do  
 As a Senior DevOps Engineer, you’ll take ownership of critical platform capabilities and help shape the future of how Solace engineers build and ship software. 
 
 Design, build, and maintain highly reliable CI/CD pipelines that support large-scale, cloud-native microservices 
 Build and maintain developer environments (local, ephemeral, and cloud-based) that are fast, secure, and easy to use 
 Define and implement Infrastructure as Code using Terraform and Helm to manage cloud resources and Kubernetes workloads 
 Partner with engineering teams to standardize deployment, testing, and release workflows 
 Improve platform observability, security, and reliability across the SDLC 
 Mentor other engineers and contribute to technical design discussions and architectural decisions 
 Drive continuous improvement by identifying friction points in the developer experience and delivering practical solutions 
 
   
  What You’ll Bring to the Role  
 
 5+ years of experience in Platform Engineering, DevOps, SRE, or related roles 
 Strong hands-on experience designing and operating CI/CD systems (e.g., GitHub Actions, Jenkins or similar) 
 Solid experience with Kubernetes, containerization, and orchestration (Docker, Helm) 
 Proven experience with Infrastructure as Code, particularly Terraform 
 Strong proficiency in at least one programming language such as Go, Python, or similar 
 Experience working in AWS or other major cloud platforms 
 Strong understanding of software delivery lifecycles, testing strategies, and release management 
 Ability to work cross-functionally and influence platform standards across teams   
 
  Nice to Have  
 
 Experience building internal developer platforms (IDPs) or self-service tooling 
 Familiarity with secrets management (e.g., HashiCorp Vault) 
 Experience improving developer experience (DevX) at scale 
 Background in highly regulated or security-conscious environments   
 
  Who You Are  
 
 A hands-on engineer who enjoys building platforms other engineers love to use 
 Passionate about automation, reliability, and developer productivity 
 Comfortable owning complex systems and driving them forward 
 Curious, pragmatic, and continuously learning 
 A collaborative team player who values high standards and shared success

*Preferably located in Ottawa, ON, open to remote Canada  
   
 #CareersThatKickSaas! TrueContext is a field intelligence platform that connects frontline workers to enterprise systems of record, enabling teams in the field to capture, act on, and learn from structured data in real time. We sit at the intersection of mobility, AI, and enterprise workflow, serving customers in utilities, oil and gas, industrial manufacturing, and other asset-intensive, regulated industries. 
   
  The Opportunity   
 The Sales Engineer is the technical face of TrueContext in every deal. You will partner directly with Account Executives to understand what prospects are trying to solve in the field, translate those problems into compelling product demonstrations, and remove every technical objection standing between a prospect and a signed contract. This is not a back-office support role. You will be in the room (or on the call) for nearly every meaningful sales conversation, shaping how customers experience our platform for the first time.  
 The right person for this role has genuine technical curiosity, can build a demo environment that mirrors a prospect’s real-world operations, and is equally comfortable presenting to a field operations director as to a CIO. You will work across utilities, oil and gas, manufacturing, and other verticals where TrueContext wins by proving that our platform solves problems other tools cannot.  
   
  What You’ll Own   
  Technical Discovery and Solution Design   
 
 Lead technical discovery calls to understand the prospect’s field operations, existing systems landscape (FSM, EAM, ERP, CRM), and integration requirements  
 
 
 Map customer business problems to TrueContext capabilities and build solution architectures that address workflow, data capture, and enterprise integration needs  
 
 
 Serve as the subject matter expert on TrueContext’s platform architecture, API and integration framework, offline capabilities, and AI features  
 
   
  Demos and Proof of Value   
 
 Build and deliver customized product demonstrations tailored to each prospect’s industry, use case, and technical environment, following structured demo methodologies (Demo2Win, Great Demo, or equivalent)  
 
 
 Develop and maintain reusable demo environments and industry-specific solution templates for priority verticals (utilities, oil and gas, industrial manufacturing)  
 
 
 Lead proof-of-concept engagements where required, defining success criteria with the prospect and managing execution to close  
 
   
  Sales Partnership and Deal Progression   
 
 Partner with Account Executives to advance opportunities through the pipeline, owning the technical win in each deal  
 
 
 Participate in RFP/RFI responses, providing accurate technical content and competitive positioning  
 
 
 Identify and address technical objections and competitive threats early, escalating product gaps to Product Management with clear business context  
 
 
 Support trade shows, industry events, and field marketing activities as a technical representative of TrueContext  
 
   
  Knowledge and Enablement   
 
 Stay current on TrueContext product releases, platform capabilities, and AI features so you can speak with authority in every conversation  
 
 
 Contribute technical content to sales enablement materials, competitive battle cards, and internal knowledge bases  
 
 
 Provide structured feedback to Product and Engineering on what you are hearing from prospects, including feature requests, integration gaps, and competitive losses  
 
   
  What We’re Looking For   
 
 3+ years of experience in a pre-sales, solutions engineering, or technical sales role in B2B SaaS, ideally selling into field operations, field service, or asset-intensive industries  
 
 
 Strong technical foundation: you can speak credibly about APIs, integrations, mobile architecture, and enterprise IT environments without needing a script  
 
 
 Demonstrated ability to build compelling, customized product demonstrations that connect platform capabilities to business outcomes  
 
 
 Experience working alongside enterprise sales teams and presenting to audiences ranging from field supervisors to CIOs  
 
 
 Comfort with ambiguity and the ability to manage multiple active deals in parallel without losing quality or follow-through  
 
 
 Fluent with AI tools (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, etc.) and uses them actively to research prospects, prepare demos, and accelerate technical work  
 
 
 Prior experience with field service platforms, mobile data capture, or workflow automation tools is a strong plus  
 
 
 Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field preferred; equivalent hands-on technical experience is equally valued  
 
   
  Why This Role   
 TrueContext is at an inflection point. We are investing in AI, expanding into new verticals, and building the sales organization to match our ambition. The Sales Engineer who joins now will have direct influence on how our platform is positioned and sold in the market, working closely with a growing team that has a clear mandate to scale. This is a high-visibility, high-impact role with real room to grow.

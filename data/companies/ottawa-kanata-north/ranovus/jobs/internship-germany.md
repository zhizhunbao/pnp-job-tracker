Join Ranovus for an Internship or to complete your Bachelor’s or Master’s thesis.     
  Location:  Nuremberg, Germany 
  Company:  Ranovus – Innovating the Future of Data Centre Connectivity 
   
  About Us  
 At  Ranovus , we’re powering the next generation of data centre infrastructure with cutting-edge fiber-optic communication technologies. With operations in  Ottawa (Canada), Nuremberg (Germany), and San Jose (USA) , we’re on a mission to deliver high-performance solutions that reduce environmental impact through lower power consumption. 
  What We Are Offering  
 We offer students who are actively pursuing a bachelor’s or master’s degree in Electrical Engineering various internship positions within our Nuremberg team. Additionally, we are open to supporting students in completing their bachelor’s or master’s thesis at Ranovus, based on an industry-relevant topic. 
  Examples of internship activities include:  
 
  Assisting in the design and simulation of full-custom analog circuits in CMOS technology. 
  Assisting in the design and simulation of full-custom integrated circuits. 
  Investigating ASIC-related CAD tools. 
  Performing characterization of existing electrical and photonic ASICs. 
  Generating system simulation models with MATLAB for transmission systems. 
  Developing electrical hardware PCBs for test circuits. 
  Measuring and characterizing optical components. 
 
 The specific topics can be agreed upon collaboratively, based on the student’s interests, university requirements, and the company’s needs. 
    
  Why Ranovus?  
 Because we don’t just build chips—we build the future. Join a team that values innovation, collaboration, and impact.

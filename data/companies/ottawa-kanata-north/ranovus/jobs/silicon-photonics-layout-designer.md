   The Opportunity      
 We are seeking a Full Time Silicon Photonics Layout Designer to develop high-quality physical layouts for advanced photonic integrated circuits. The ideal candidate has strong experience using Cadence layout tools and a solid understanding of LVS/DRC flows for photonic designs. You will work closely with photonic device engineers, circuit designers, and process teams to translate schematics and specifications into manufacturable layouts for silicon photonics platforms. 
   
   How You’ll Contribute      
 
 Create and maintain photonic layouts using Cadence (Virtuoso) and foundry PDKs. 
 Implement waveguide routing, passive and electro-optic devices (rings, MZIs, couplers, modulators, detectors). 
 Ensure layout compliance with foundry design rules (DRC) and perform LVS between schematic and layout. 
 Debug and resolve DRC/LVS issues, including photonic-specific connectivity and hierarchy challenges. 
 Integrate optical and electrical components in compact, manufacturable floorplans. 
 Collaborate with design, modeling, and process teams to optimize layouts for performance, yield, and reliability. 
 Prepare tape-out-ready GDS files and support signoff flows. 
 Develop and improve layout methodologies, templates, and automation where applicable. 
 Document layout guidelines and contribute to best practices for PIC design. 
 
   
   What You’ll Bring      
 
 Bachelor’s or master’s degree in electrical engineering, Photonics, Physics, or a related field. 
 3+ years of experience in IC or photonic layout design. 
 Strong proficiency with Cadence Virtuoso for layout. 
 Hands-on experience running and debugging DRC and LVS. 
 Familiarity with silicon photonics components and layouts (waveguides, rings, MZIs, grating couplers, heaters, PN junctions). 
 Understanding of hierarchical layout, parameterized cells (PCells), and foundry PDK usage. 
 Experience with mixed photonic/electrical layouts is a plus. 
 Ability to interpret schematics, netlists, and design specifications. 
 Strong attention to detail and ability to manage complex layouts across large designs. 
 
   
  Nice to Have  
 
 Experience with photonic LVS frameworks or custom connectivity checks. 
 Scripting experience (Python, SKILL, MATLAB) for layout automation. 
 Familiarity with multiple foundries or photonics PDKs (e.g., GF, TSMC, IME, Tower Jazz). 
 Experience supporting silicon photonics tape-outs and post-silicon debug.

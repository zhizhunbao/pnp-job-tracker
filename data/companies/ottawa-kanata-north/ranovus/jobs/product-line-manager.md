Product Line Manager (PLM) – Optical Engines   
      
   About Us      
   Ranovus   is     a semiconductor company with     global operations     helping power the data behind next         generation AI and data center networks. We design and build advanced hardware that moves massive amounts of information quickly and efficiently between computer systems in data centers around the world. Our technology improves performance while reducing energy use and environmental impact, supporting the rapid growth of AI and cloud services. At   Ranovus  ,   you’ll   join a collaborative, inclusive team that values learning, flexibility, and meaningful work with global impact.      
   
  The Opportunity  
 We are seeking a    Product Line Manager (PLM)  to own the strategy, roadmap, and execution for our next-generation    multi-terabit optical engines . 
   
   How You’ll Contribute      
 
  Product Strategy &amp; Roadmap 
 
 Define and execute the product roadmap for high-capacity optical engines (100G–200G/lane, multi-terabit systems). 
 Anticipate customer needs and align products to Hyperscaler, AI/ML infrastructure, and networking roadmaps. 
 Work with executive leadership to set pricing, positioning, and go-to-market strategy. 
 
 
  Customer &amp; Market Engagement 
 
 Act as the primary interface with hyperscalers, networking OEMs, and semiconductor partners. 
 Collect and translate customer requirements into product features and specifications. 
 Build business cases, market analyses, and competitive positioning for product decisions. 
 
 
  Cross-Functional Leadership 
 
 Drive collaboration across engineering, sales, operations, and executive teams to deliver on time and within budget. 
 Define MRDs (Market Requirement Documents) and PRDs (Product Requirement Documents). 
 Ensure seamless new product introduction (NPI) and lifecycle management. 
 
 
  Performance &amp; Metrics 
 
 Track KPIs across revenue, margins, adoption rate, and customer satisfaction. 
 Identify opportunities for differentiation in power efficiency, bandwidth density, and cost per bit. 
 
 
 
   
    What You’ll Bring       
 
 Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering, Physics, Computer Engineering, or related field (Master’s or MBA preferred). 
 5+ years of experience in product management, business development, or technical marketing in optical interconnects, photonics, or semiconductor industries. 
 Strong technical understanding of silicon photonics, optical engines, pluggables, or Co-Packaged Optics (CPO). 
 Proven success managing multi-terabit interconnect products or high-speed networking technologies (Ethernet, PCIe, InfiniBand, etc.). 
 Excellent communication, presentation, and negotiation skills with ability to engage C-level and technical stakeholders. 
 Entrepreneurial mindset with a track record of driving new product adoption in emerging markets.

Join Ranovus as an ASIC Validation Engineer  (All Experience Levels Welcome!)  
  Location:  Nuremberg, Germany 
  Team:  ASIC validation team 
  Company:  Ranovus – Innovating the Future of Data Center Connectivity 
   
  About Us  
 At  Ranovus , we’re powering the next generation of data center infrastructure with cutting-edge fiber-optic communication technologies. With operations in  Ottawa (Canada), Nuremberg (Germany), and San Jose (USA) , we’re on a mission to deliver high-performance solutions that reduce environmental impact through lower power consumption. 
   
  What You’ll Do  
 As part of our ASIC validation team, you’ll help ensuring Ranovus ASICs meet the customer requirements. 
 
 Develop and execute  validation plans  for photonic and electrical ASICs at system and chip level. 
 Perform  lab-based testing and characterization  of photonic and electrical ASICs using oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, VNA and other measurement equipment. 
 Debug and analyze  failures on silicon , using oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, and other lab tools. 
 Collaborate with design, verification, and software teams to resolve issues. 
 Automate validation workflows using  Python, Perl, Shell or Matlab scripts . 
 Document test results and provide  clear reports  to stakeholders. 
 Support  bring-up of new ASICs  and system integration. 
 
    
  What You Bring  We’re hiring across experience levels, so whether you’re just starting out or a seasoned expert, we’d love to hear from you. Ideal candidates will have: 
 
 Hands-on experience with lab characterization and RF measurements 
 
  Experience with on-die measurement with  probe cards . 
 Familiarity with  lab equipment.  
 Proficiency in  scripting  for automation. 
 Team spirit and excellent communication 
 Background in full custom analog/mixed-signal IC design 
  Fluency in English  
 
   
  Why Ranovus?  
 Because we don’t just build chips—we build the future. Join a team that values innovation, collaboration, and impact.

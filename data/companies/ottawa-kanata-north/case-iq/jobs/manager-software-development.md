Case IQ is a global leader in compliance and risk management solutions, providing innovative software that empowers organizations to effectively manage and mitigate risks related to fraud, corruption, sanctions, kickbacks, and conflicts of interest. With over 1,100 customers worldwide, Case IQ serves a diverse range of organizations from small businesses to large enterprises. Founded in 1999, the company operates with a remote-first workforce across the US, Canada, Europe, and APAC. 
   
 In a strategic expansion, Case IQ acquired Lextegrity, enhancing its offerings to create a comprehensive, end-to-end compliance solution. The unified platform now includes advanced whistleblowing solutions, robust case management tools, and a suite of compliance tools for approvals, disclosures, third-party risk management, and real-time compliance monitoring. By integrating real-time risk detection and AI-driven insights, Case IQ sets a new standard in compliance management, enabling faster investigations, more impactful outcomes, and enhanced operational efficiency. 
   
  Position Summary  
 We are seeking a dynamic Development Manager to lead our engineering team, focusing on our Compliance Monitoring product. This role is pivotal in guiding the technical direction and ensuring the successful delivery of high-quality software solutions. The ideal candidate will possess strong technical expertise, particularly in Python, Apache Airflow, and database technologies like Snowflake or AWS RDS on Postgres, and will be adept at providing technical guidance and mentorship to the engineering team. 
   
  Please note: while this is a remote position, we are looking for candidates in an Eastern or Central Timezone.   
   
  What You'll Do  
 
 Leadership and Mentorship: Lead and mentor engineering team members through regular 1:1s, feedback, and career development planning, fostering a culture of engineering excellence and continuous learning. 
 Technical Guidance: Review pull requests and provide technical guidance to engineers, ensuring best practices in coding and architecture are followed. 
 Cross-Team Coordination: Remove obstacles and coordinate cross-team dependencies to ensure smooth project execution. 
 Collaboration: Collaborate with product leadership on roadmap planning and technical strategy, aligning engineering efforts with business objectives. 
 Development Participation: Participate directly in development activities to maintain technical expertise and support the team. 
 Team Management: Manage hiring, performance evaluations, and professional development opportunities to build a high-performing team. 
 Delivery and Quality Management: Drive team delivery and quality while managing capacity and workload across team members. 
 Process Improvement: Oversee sprint planning, execution, and process improvements to enhance team productivity and efficiency. 
 
   
  What We're Looking For  
 
 Education and Experience: Bachelor’s degree in computer science, engineering, or a related field, or equivalent work experience. 7+ years in software development, with 3+ years in a leadership or management role. 
 Technical Expertise: Extensive experience with Python, Apache Airflow, and database technologies such as Snowflake or AWS RDS on Postgres. 
 Technical Leadership: Proven ability to provide technical guidance and mentorship, with a strong track record of reviewing code and guiding engineering teams. 
 Project Management: Experience in managing software development projects, with a focus on agile methodologies and continuous improvement. 
 Communication and Collaboration: Excellent communication skills, capable of working well within a team and engaging with stakeholders across the organization. 
 Exercise the highest level of discretion in the handling and protection of all data and sensitive information.

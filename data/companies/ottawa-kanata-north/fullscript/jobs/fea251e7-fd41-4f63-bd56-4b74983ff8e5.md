About Fullscript
 
We’re an industry-leading health technology company on a mission to help people get better. We started in 2011 with one simple idea. Make it easier for practitioners to access the products they trust so they can deliver better care.
 
That simple idea grew into a platform that powers every part of care. Today, more than 125,000 practitioners use Fullscript for clinical insights, lab interpretations, patient analytics, education, and access to high-quality supplements. Over 10 million patients rely on Fullscript to stay connected to their care plans and follow through on treatment.
 
We build tools that make care smarter and more human. Tools that save time, simplify decisions, and help practitioners stay closely connected to the people they care for. When everything they need is in one place, they can focus on what matters most: helping people get better.
 
This is your invitation.
 
Bring your ideas, your grit, and your care for people.
Join us and shape the future of care.

The Role
Fullscript is looking for a Senior Product Designer (Mobile App), to help shape the future of our patient experience, with a primary focus on our mobile app (iOS & Android). 
This role sits at the center of the patient experience, focused on the commerce moments that matter most: ordering, refills, subscriptions, account management, and overall app look and feel. These experiences need to feel fast, intuitive, trustworthy, and polished, on par with the best consumer products patients use every day.
But this is not a traditional e-commerce role. At Fullscript, commerce is closely connected to care. Patients may be following provider recommendations, managing routines, or refilling products they rely on. The right designer will bring strong commerce instincts while keeping that care context in mind.
You’ll partner closely with Product and Engineering to define, design, and ship patient experiences end-to-end, primarily in the mobile app and occasionally across the web platform. You can be empowered to find opportunities or problems and jump right in to fix them. We trust our teams to use their expertise to continually raise the bar of our product and act like an owner.
⚠️ A portfolio is required to be considered for this role.

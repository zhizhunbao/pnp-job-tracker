Who We Are; What We Do; Where We’re Going
 
Magnet Forensics is a global leader in the development of digital investigative software that acquires, analyzes, and shares evidence from computers, smartphones, tablets, and IoT-related devices. We are continually innovating so our customers can deploy advanced and effective tools to protect their companies, communities, and countries.
 
Serving thousands of customers globally, our solutions are playing a crucial role in modernizing digital investigations, helping investigators fight crime, protect assets, and guard national security.
 
With employees based around the world, Magnet Forensics has been expanding our global presence. As a part of Magnet Forensics, you can expect to make a difference in the world, no matter what role you play. You’ll be supported through learning and development, not to mention an incredible team with unbelievable talent and integrity. 
 
If you think you would be the right person to join our team working towards this goal, we would love to hear from you! 

Role Overview 
 
Magnet Forensics is seeking a high-performing Enterprise Account Executive to drive new business growth across the DACH region within the Digital Forensics and Incident Response market.
 
This role is focused on building pipeline, acquiring strategic new enterprise customers, and expanding adoption across corporate and private-sector organisations. The successful candidate will demonstrate a proven ability to independently develop territory strategy, create opportunities, and own complex enterprise sales cycles end-to-end using a MEDDPICC-driven sales approach.
 
As a key member of Magnet Forensics’ EMEA team, you will operate with significant ownership of the DACH region, working closely with partners, marketing, sales engineering, and leadership to establish and scale market presence.
 
This role is best suited to candidates motivated by long-term territory development and market ownership rather than short-term account management or inherited revenue.
 
Candidate must reside in Germany

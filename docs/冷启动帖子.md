## 2. 一亩三分地(求职/移民版,中文,干货长帖)

**标题**:[分享] 做了个免费职位板:全加拿大 3 万+在招岗位 × PNP/EE/LMIA 信号逐岗标注,每小时更新

**正文**:

地里潜水多年,第一次发帖。背景:在加数据工程师,身边 PGWP 的朋友找工作太苦了,决定把「找工作」和「查移民路径」这两件事合成一件。

**站点**:offer2pr.com(公测,基础功能免费,无需注册就能看全部职位)

**它解决什么**:找工作时你真正想知道的其实是——这个岗位/这家公司,对我拿 PR 有没有用?但这个答案散在十几个官网里:各省 PNP 职业清单、IRCC 的 EE 类别抽选、ESDC 的 LMIA 获批记录和工资中位数……一个个查一晚上就没了。

**具体功能**(全部标注官方来源,可逐条核):
1. 全加拿大 10 省全职业在招岗位(Job Bank 及其聚合源),每小时增量抓取,现在 3 万+
2. 每行直接标:职业是否命中省提名清单(具体到 stream,如 SK 科技/AB 科技)、是否在联邦 EE 定向类别(带最近抽分和邀请数)、雇主是否 AIP 指定雇主
3. 雇主 LMIA 史:ESDC 季度公开数据按公司名归一匹配,能看到「这家公司近两年获批过 N 个外劳职位、什么股别」——判断雇主愿不愿意办手续,比 Glassdoor 有用
4. 工资 vs 中位:每岗年薪折算后对比该 NOC×省的 ESDC 中位数
5. 「最可能担保雇主榜」:按 LMIA 获批职位数排的 TOP30,找 sponsor 友好型雇主直接从这看
6. 建档案(NOC/CLB/CRS/目标省)后逐岗算匹配度,给依据链(哪条清单命中/差多少分),不是黑箱打分
7. 地区统计:按省×职业大类看岗位量/中位薪资/清单岗数量,选省用
8. 岗位收藏+投递进度跟踪(想投/已投/面试中/offer),账户里「我的求职」统一管理(免费)

**几句实话**:
- 我不是 RCIC,站上所有结论都只到「符合/不符合公开清单条件」为止,不构成移民建议
- 数据来源全部是政府公开数据,每个数字都带来源链接
- 聚合帖的正文格式偶尔不完美(Job Bank 源头压平的),在持续修
- 有 Pro 档(CA$19/30 天,解锁工资中位对比列、无限匹配等),但基础功能长期免费

求反馈:哪个功能没用/缺什么/哪里看不懂,回帖或站内页脚邮箱都行,基本当天改。

## 3. Reddit(英文;候选 sub:r/ImmigrationCanada、r/CanadaJobs、r/PGWP —— **发前逐个读版规**,self-promo 严格的 sub 走每周 thread 或先攒 karma)

**Title**: I built a free job board that flags which Canadian jobs can actually lead to PR (PNP list hits, EE category draws, employer LMIA history)

**Body**:

Data engineer in Canada here. Watched too many friends on PGWP burn months applying to jobs that could never support their immigration path — the employer had zero LMIA history, or the occupation wasn't on any PNP list.

So I built offer2pr.com — a free job board that pulls 30k+ live postings across all 10 provinces (from Job Bank and its aggregated sources, refreshed hourly) and annotates each one with the immigration signals you'd otherwise dig through a dozen government sites to find:

- **PNP list hit**: is this NOC on a provincial nominee stream list right now (e.g. SK Tech, AB Tech) — flagged per row
- **EE category**: is the occupation in a federal Express Entry category-based draw, with the latest cutoff score and invitation count
- **Employer LMIA history**: has this employer actually had positive LMIAs in the last 2 years (ESDC quarterly open data, matched by employer name — historical fact, not a promise)
- **Wage vs median**: posted salary vs the official ESDC median for that NOC × province
- A "most likely to sponsor" employer ranking based on approved LMIA position counts
- Save jobs and track your application pipeline (to-apply / applied / interviewing / offer) — free

You can also set up a profile (NOC, CLB, CRS) and it scores every job against your situation with a visible reasoning chain ("last EE draw cutoff was 475, you're at 480") instead of a black-box score.

Full transparency: I'm not an immigration consultant and this is not immigration advice — every data point links to its official source so you can verify. The site is in public beta, core features are free, no signup needed to browse. Feedback very welcome; I ship fixes daily.

## 2. 一亩三分地(求职版优先,中文,干货长帖)

**标题**:[分享] 做了个免费职位板:全加拿大 3 万+ 在招岗位,每小时更新;顺带把 PNP/EE/LMIA 信号逐岗标了

**正文**:

地里潜水多年,第一次发帖。背景:在加数据工程师,自己和身边朋友找工作都苦于「岗位太散、信息不全」,就动手做了个聚合板,顺手把大家找工作时最难自己查的移民信号也标上了。

**站点**:offer2pr.com(公测,基础功能免费,无需注册就能看全部职位)

**先说它作为「职位板」本身**:
1. 全加拿大 10 省全职业在招岗位(Job Bank 及其聚合源),每小时增量抓取,现在 3 万 1 千+
2. 一个板看全国全职业,按省 / 城市 / 职业大类 / 薪资筛,搜索也快
3. 每岗工资 vs 中位:年薪折算后对比该 NOC×省 的 ESDC 官方中位数——判断 offer 值不值
4. 岗位收藏 + 投递进度跟踪(想投 / 已投 / 面试中 / offer),账户里「我的求职」统一管理(免费)
5. 地区统计:按省×职业大类看岗位量 / 中位薪资,挑城市 / 挑方向用

**再说别的招聘站没有的「移民信号」层**(全部标注官方来源,可逐条核):
6. 每行直接标:职业是否命中省提名清单(具体到 stream,如 SK 科技 / AB 科技)、是否在联邦 EE 定向类别(带最近抽分和邀请数)
7. 雇主 LMIA 史:ESDC 季度公开数据按公司名归一匹配,能看到「这家公司近两年获批过 N 个外劳职位、什么股别」——判断雇主愿不愿意办手续,比 Glassdoor 有用
8. 「最可能担保雇主榜」:按 LMIA 获批职位数排的 TOP30
9. 建档案(NOC/CLB/CRS/目标省)后逐岗算匹配度,给依据链(哪条清单命中 / 差多少分),不是黑箱打分

**几句实话**:
- 这首先是个找工作的工具;移民信号是加分层,我不是 RCIC,站上所有移民相关结论只到「符合 / 不符合公开清单条件」为止,不构成移民建议
- 数据来源全部是政府公开数据,每个数字都带来源链接
- 聚合帖的正文格式偶尔不完美(Job Bank 源头压平的),在持续修
- 有 Pro 档(CA$19/30 天,解锁工资中位对比列、无限匹配等),但基础功能长期免费

求反馈:哪个功能没用 / 缺什么 / 哪里看不懂,回帖或站内页脚邮箱都行,基本当天改。

## 3. Reddit(英文;候选 sub:r/CanadaJobs、r/jobs、r/ImmigrationCanada —— **发前逐个读版规**,self-promo 严格的 sub 走每周 thread 或先攒 karma)

**Title**: I built a free job board for Canada — 30k+ live postings across all provinces, refreshed hourly (and it also flags which jobs help with PR)

**Body**:

Data engineer in Canada here. Job hunting was a pain — postings scattered across Indeed, LinkedIn and a dozen company sites, and you never really know if a salary is any good. So I built offer2pr.com, a free job board that tries to make the search less painful.

**As a job board:**
- 30k+ live postings across all 10 provinces (from Job Bank and its aggregated sources), refreshed hourly
- One place for all occupations; filter by province / city / occupation / salary
- Posted salary vs the official ESDC median for that occupation × province, so you can tell if an offer is fair
- Save jobs and track your application pipeline (to-apply / applied / interviewing / offer) — free
- Regional stats: where the jobs and higher wages are, by province and occupation group

**Plus a layer no other job board has** — if you also care about your PR path, every posting is annotated with immigration signals (all linked to official sources):
- Whether the occupation is on a provincial nominee (PNP) stream list, or in a federal Express Entry category-based draw (with the latest cutoff and invitation count)
- Employer LMIA history from ESDC quarterly open data (a historical fact, not a promise)
- A searchable **employer directory** (offer2pr.com/employers): look up any employer's LMIA approval record or AIP designation — the stuff you'd otherwise dig out of quarterly government spreadsheets
- Optional profile (NOC, CLB, CRS) that scores each job against your situation with a visible reasoning chain instead of a black-box score

Full transparency: it's first and foremost a job board; the immigration part is a helper layer. I'm not an immigration consultant and this isn't immigration advice — every data point links to its official source. Public beta, core features free, no signup needed to browse. Feedback very welcome; I ship fixes daily.

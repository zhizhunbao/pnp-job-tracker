## §1 竞品/替代品差评(35 条 + 旅程花费盘点)

### A. Job Bank(jobbank.gc.ca)官方平台

**CP#1. 岗位大量是"LMIA合规性挂牌",雇主根本不想招人**
- 竞品:Job Bank
- 证据:moving2canada 专文回应 reddit 上的普遍抱怨——"Many newcomers claim the Job Bank is useless, full of scams, or only used by employers trying to obtain LMIAs";文章承认"some postings may exist solely to satisfy regulatory requirements, meaning the employer may not seriously consider outside applicants"(https://moving2canada.com/features/canada-job-bank-legit-or-scam/)
- 定价证据:雇主为走 LMIA 必须公开登广告≥28天,这一合规动作本身就是海量幽灵岗位的来源

**CP#2. 新移民在 reddit 上直接称 Job Bank 为"useless / full of scams"**
- 竞品:Job Bank
- 证据:moving2canada 汇总 reddit 原话(同上链接);官方以"legit but not efficient"回应,等于承认体验差
- 定价证据:求职者付出的是时间成本——每一份投给合规挂牌岗位的申请都是沉没成本

**CP#3. 政府平台却不做雇主核验,真假岗位混杂,辨别责任全推给求职者**
- 竞品:Job Bank
- 证据:moving2canada 列出需自查的红旗:"Personal email addresses (Gmail, Yahoo) instead of business domains""Missing or suspicious company online presence""Salaries significantly above regional median rates"
- 定价证据:无直接金额;但辨别失败的下游就是 $2K-$200K 的 LMIA 骗局(见 CP#24-27)

**CP#4. 骗子用"虚高工资岗位"套高薪 LMIA 流,实际不发或少发工资**
- 竞品:Job Bank / LMIA 体系
- 证据:JobWatchCanada:"listing roles at inflated wages—such as $36 per hour for low-skill roles like cooks or food supervisors—to qualify for less restrictive high-wage LMIA streams, while actually paying workers far less or nothing"(https://jobwatchcanada.com/the-scam)
- 定价证据:受害者面临"low or no pay, wage repayments"(先拿工资再退还给雇主)

**CP#5. 雇主长期不回复申请者,严重到政府要靠"关停不回复账号"来治理**
- 竞品:Job Bank
- 证据:JobWatchCanada:"Job Bank now monitors employer engagement with applicants, closing accounts of those who ignore submissions"——需要专项整治,说明"投了没人理"是系统性痼疾
- 定价证据:时间成本;LMIA 挂牌期内雇主可用"applicants didn't respond to contact attempts"等借口打发本地申请者

### B. Indeed / LinkedIn(对移民求职者)

**CP#6. LinkedIn 没有 visa sponsorship 筛选器,只能靠关键词碰运气**
- 竞品:LinkedIn
- 证据:Quora/Workbeyond 对比文:"LinkedIn doesn't offer a visa sponsorship filter"(https://www.workbeyond.com/resources/linkedin-vs-indeed-vs-seek-vs-workbeyond-which-platform-works-for-international-careers-in-2026)
- 定价证据:催生了 Workbeyond 这类付费"verified sponsor"平台,证明用户愿为此付费

**CP#7. Indeed 的 sponsorship 标签是算法自动打的,"widely reported by candidates as inconsistent"**
- 竞品:Indeed
- 证据:同上,"Indeed's visa sponsorship tag is auto-tagged by the algorithm…it does not verify whether the employer actually holds a sponsor licence, whether they are currently exercising it, or whether the specific role would be sponsored"
- 定价证据:每次误投=一份定制简历+cover letter 的时间;付费简历服务按 $99-$500/份计价(见 CP#33)

**CP#8. "must be legally eligible to work in Canada / no sponsorship" 藏在职位描述深处,投完/面完才发现**
- 竞品:Indeed / LinkedIn 全体主流板
- 证据:搜索结果显示大量帖子写明"Candidates with the right to work in Canada without visa sponsorship""Visa sponsorship is not available",且无结构化字段可提前过滤(https://ca.indeed.com/q-linkedin-visa-sponsorship-jobs.html)
- 定价证据:浪费的申请数量级——56% 加拿大求职者疑似投过幽灵岗(见下条)

**CP#9. 加拿大约31%的招聘帖是"ghost job"(根本不打算招人)**
- 竞品:Indeed / LinkedIn / 各大板
- 证据:jobs.ca 对 10,247 个岗位的分析:"31% of job postings are ghost jobs";Employment Hero 调查:56% 加拿大求职者疑似申过幽灵岗(https://www.jobs.ca/blogs/ghost-jobs-in-canada-2025-31-of-job-postings-are-fake-complete-detection-guide、https://employmenthero.com/en-ca/blog/canadians-ghost-jobs-survey/)
- 定价证据:ResumeBuilder 2024:40% 公司承认当年发过假岗、30% 当时挂着假岗

### C. LMIA 信息层(官方数据可用性)

**CP#10. 官方"正面 LMIA 雇主清单"是季度 Excel,滞后、排除个人雇主名、明确声明不完整**
- 竞品:open.canada.ca 官方数据
- 证据:官方说明:"the list…excludes all personal names…so the list is not complete and does not reflect all employers who requested or received an LMIA"(https://open.canada.ca/data/en/dataset/90fed587-1364-4f33-a9ee-208181dc0b97)
- 定价证据:免费但不可用性高,催生 LMIA Finder、canadahires 等第三方封装工具

**CP#11. 清单上的雇主≠现在还能/还愿意办 LMIA(低薪帽、违规、配额)**
- 竞品:LMIA 雇主清单及其套壳站
- 证据:Indeed 职业建议页:"These companies may not be actively hiring…not all listed may still be eligible to hire workers due to…the cap on hiring for low-wage workers, compliance violations or unemployment rates"(https://ca.indeed.com/career-advice/finding-a-job/list-of-lmia-companies-in-canada)
- 定价证据:无;这正是"历史LMIA信号+当前在招岗位"交叉验证的产品空位

**CP#12. 求职者无法预先知道"哪个雇主愿意支持 PNP/LMIA",只能海投硬撞**
- 竞品:所有现有工具
- 证据:160,686 家历史 LMIA 雇主、1,410,914 条批准记录存在(applywave 统计),但没有工具把它和实时岗位、和"我的省/我的NOC"连起来
- 定价证据:这块信息差正是黑市卖 $25K-45K"带LMIA工作"的定价基础(CP#24)

**CP#13. 雇主端被 LMIA 流程劝退:28天广告+6周起步+繁重文书+$1000政府费**
- 竞品:LMIA 制度本身(影响求职者拿offer概率)
- 证据:多家律所指南:"LMIA applications are extremely detailed and require a lot of documentation…the entire process takes a minimum of six weeks"(https://www.immigrationcare.ca/blog/understanding-lmia-process-step-step-guide-employers-canada)
- 定价证据:LMIA 政府费 $1,000/申请(依法必须雇主承担,不得转嫁——但现实中被转嫁成 CP#24 的黑市价)

**CP#14. 2024年底收紧后新 LMIA 申请暴跌约90%,存量"LMIA岗位"信息更稀缺更值钱**
- 竞品:整个 LMIA 岗位信息生态
- 证据:JobWatchCanada:"New LMIA applications declined approximately 90% year-over-year following tightened regulations"
- 定价证据:稀缺性推高黑市价格与咨询费

### D. 移民顾问(RCIC)与律师

**CP#15. 全代理收费高:Express Entry $3,000-5,000,PNP 最高 $10,000**
- 竞品:RCIC 顾问
- 证据:"For Express Entry with full representation, RCIC fees typically range from $3,000 to $5,000 CAD…$1,000–$10,000 for PNP applications"(https://visaguru.ca/how-much-do-immigration-consultants-charge-in-canada-a-complete-2025-guide/、https://immimentor.ca/2026/04/02/a-guide-to-rcic-fees-ranges-and-what-affects-them/)
- 定价证据:同上;且 CICC 无官方费率表,"fair and reasonable"全凭顾问自定

**CP#16. 律师 $200-500/小时,单次咨询 $100-300/半小时到一小时**
- 竞品:移民律师
- 证据:"consultation fees ranging from $100 to $300 for a 30–60 minute session…hourly rates between $200–$500/hour"(https://visarete.com/how-much-does-an-immigration-lawyer-cost/、https://www.fisher-law.ca/fisher-law-fees:$250-400/hr)
- 定价证据:问一个"我的岗位算不算某PNP流"级别的问题就要 $100-300

**CP#17. 付款前殷勤、付款后失联**
- 竞品:移民咨询公司(Trustpilot 差评)
- 证据:Trustpilot 原话:"when they talk to you its like honey but if you paid money after that no response"(https://www.trustpilot.com/review/www.migrationsconsultants.com)
- 定价证据:该客户 AIPP 申请付款后 6 个月零进展

**CP#18. 进度黑洞:承诺9个月完成后再无回音;访问签证被拖2年**
- 竞品:DM-Immigration、CanadaCIS 等
- 证据:"Clients reported no response after being told they would finish the application process within 9 months"(https://www.trustpilot.com/review/dm-consultant.com);"waiting two years for response on visiting visas that typically take 2 to 8 weeks"(https://ca.trustpilot.com/review/www.canadacis.org)
- 定价证据:此类公司收费通常数千加元/案

**CP#19. "分配给你的顾问电话都不接",退款扯皮**
- 竞品:Abroad Pathway 等
- 证据:"the consultants that they assign to your account hardly pick your call"+退款投诉(https://ca.trustpilot.com/review/www.abroadpathway.com)
- 定价证据:预付制、退款需自行追讨

**CP#20. 持牌顾问也会卖"不存在的项目":温哥华 Lucion 案,数十受害者每人 ~$5,000+**
- 竞品:持牌 RCIC
- 证据:CBC:"advertised and charged exorbitant fees to file immigration applications through a program that did not exist…dozens of victims paid fees of roughly $5,000 or more"(https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/dozens-of-victims-allege-vancouver-consultant-scammed-them-with-fake-immigration-scheme-1.6676811)
- 定价证据:~$5,000+/人 × 数十人

**CP#21. "Ghost consultancy"(无牌幽灵中介)泛滥:Windsor 男子为妻子签证被骗 $12K**
- 竞品:无牌中介
- 证据:CBC 标题即证据:"'Rising problem' of ghost consultancies hits man who lost $12K trying to get Canadian visa for wife"(https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/windsor/rising-problem-of-ghost-consultancies-in-canada-1.7485089)
- 定价证据:$12,000/单案

**CP#22. 出事了投诉无门追不回钱:CICC 只查行为不追款,退款要自己上小额法庭**
- 竞品:RCIC 监管体系
- 证据:"the CICC does not investigate complaints to recover money, it investigates to enforce the Code of Professional Conduct"(https://college-ic.ca/protecting-the-public/complaints-process 相关指南)
- 定价证据:联邦到 2026-07-15 才上线顾问赔偿基金(refdesk.ca),此前受害者基本无兜底

**CP#23. 顾问名字被伪造在假 LMIA 文件上,受害者付了钱拿到的是废纸**
- 竞品:文书黑市
- 证据:CBC:"B.C. immigration consultant discovers forged foreign worker documents — with her name on them"(https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/british-columbia/forged-foreign-worker-documents-1.7516048)
- 定价证据:假文件按真 LMIA 打包价出售(见下节)

### E. LMIA"工作offer打包出售"黑市(用户真实付费上限的标尺)

**CP#24. 网上15分钟就能找到公开出售的"带LMIA工作",标价最高 $45,000**
- 竞品:社媒/分类广告黑市
- 证据:CBC 调查标题:"We found fake jobs for foreigners illegally for sale online in 15 minutes…offering fake jobs to foreign workers in exchange for up to $45,000"(https://www.cbc.ca/news/investigates/cbc-ijf-lmia-ads-investigation-1.7350596)
- 定价证据:$25K-45K 为公开报价区间

**CP#25. Alberta:打工者为一份工作被收 $30K+ 非法费用;个案分期付 $40K;极端 $75K**
- 竞品:雇主+中介+招聘者合谋链条
- 证据:CBC:"the owner of an Edmonton-based immigration business was convicted for charging $30,000 and $45,000 to arrange employment…Sheikh paid roughly $40,000 in intervals…Some cases involve fees up to $75,000",移民律师称之为"a form of pseudo slavery"(https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/calgary/temporary-foreign-workers-scam-1.7254863)
- 定价证据:$30K-75K;而合法 LMIA 政府费仅 $1,000 且必须雇主出

**CP#26. 多伦多 WonHonTa:$170K-180K 买假工作,自己给自己发工资,打款到个人账户避税**
- 竞品:移民"就业"公司
- 证据:CBC 卧底:"it's $180,000 for a job in Saskatchewan or $170,000 for the Atlantic provinces…the would-be immigrant would be buying the job and paying their own wage"(https://www.cbc.ca/news/canada/saskatchewan/immigration-jobs-fees-china-canada-1.5272394)
- 定价证据:$170K-180K/单

**CP#27. 骗局价格带全景:$2,000 到 $200,000**
- 竞品:整个"guaranteed job offer/work permit"黑市
- 证据:JobWatchCanada:"Foreign workers are charged $2,000 to $200,000 for fraudulent 'guaranteed' job offers or work permits"
- 定价证据:同上——这就是"真·可信岗位信号"的支付意愿天花板

**CP#28. 封闭工签把人锁死在雇主身上:换工作=新LMIA+新工签,付过钱的人更不敢维权**
- 竞品:TFW 制度结构
- 证据:"Because temporary foreign workers' permits are tied to their employers, and they've already spent thousands to get the job, they can be more vulnerable to further abuse"(CBC Calgary 同上;IRCC help centre qnum=1201)
- 定价证据:换雇主的隐性成本=再来一轮 LMIA(6周+)+工签申请费

### F. 免费工具(CRS计算器 / draw tracker / 资讯站)

**CP#29. CRS 计算器只吐一个分数,不告诉你"这个分数意味着什么、下一步做什么"**
- 竞品:canadavisa、moving2canada、crscalculate 等各家计算器
- 证据:各家免责声明自认:"calculator results do not take into account all possible factors…not always 100% accurate"(https://moving2canada.com/immigration/express-entry/crs-score/express-entry-crs-calculator/ 等)
- 定价证据:免费;"分数→行动方案"的解释目前只能花 $100-300 问律师/顾问(CP#16)

**CP#30. Draw tracker 全是"事后新闻",预测纯靠历史外推,政策一变即作废**
- 竞品:CIC News、ircc.com tracker、immigrationnewscanada 等
- 证据:tracker 自己写:"No private website, calculator or tracker knows the next draw date, cut-off, or category in advance…Anything labelled a 'prediction' is an estimate based on past patterns, and patterns break whenever IRCC changes draw size, cadence or policy"(https://ircc.com/express-entry/draw-tracker)
- 定价证据:免费+广告/导流变现;无个性化,不回答"我该走哪条路"

**CP#31. 80+个 PNP 流、11个省区各自为政,没有任何工具做"我的档案↔流"匹配**
- 竞品:各省官网+Canadim 等百科站
- 证据:"there are more than 80 active PNP streams across 11 provinces and territories…Each province designs its own streams, sets its own eligibility criteria"(https://www.canadim.com/immigrate/provincial-nominee-program/);且流频繁改版(OINP 2026-05-30 九流全废改四通道)
- 定价证据:这块复杂度正是 RCIC 收 $1,000-10,000/PNP案 的收入来源

**CP#32. PNP 材料复杂,"submitted incorrectly or incomplete"直接拒——免费信息帮不到执行层**
- 竞品:官网+百科站
- 证据:Canadim:"The PNP application process can be confusing, and applications can be refused if they are submitted incorrectly or if they are incomplete"
- 定价证据:一次被拒=省级申请费打水漂+重排队;2025年 PNP 联邦配额还被砍半("reduced by about 50% for 2025",myvisasource)

### G. 文档/考试/求职服务(旅程上的既有付费点)

- 定价证据:$322-410/次 × 多次尝试(语言分是 CRS 最大可控变量,多次重考普遍)

**CP#34. WES 认证名义30个工作日,实际全程6-12周,丢件+客服升级无果的差评成堆**

- 定价证据:评估费 $264 CAD,含税+快递全包 $330-390

**CP#35. "Canadian-style resume"再收割一轮:改简历 $99-995,面试教练 $100-300/小时**
- 竞品:TopResume、Resume Target、Careers by Design 等
- 证据:"Resume packages in Canada start at $299 CAD"(resumetarget);"$99 起"(topresume.ca);"Find My Profession $395–$995 USD"(findmyprofession);教练"$90 to $300+ per hour"(persuasiveinterview/bark)
- 定价证据:同上;背景是"用母国简历格式基本收不到回复"的普遍建议(RBC 新移民指南)

### A. 规划路线

**EN#2. PGWP 临期时的备选路径(LMIA/C10/法语/回国攒海外经验)组合极复杂,只能发帖求"所有可能的 backup options"**

- 强度:频(同类帖每周多条)+烈;该用户已聘移民律师(钱)

**EN#3. CRS 500+ 也可能因抽签间隔太长而空等到语言成绩过期,「分数够」≠「能上岸」没人提前告诉他**
